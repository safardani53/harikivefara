Why is 'Ty Burrell death' trending?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, March 29, 2024

TY Burrell is well-known for having starred on the hit ABC sitcom, Modern Family.

Now, fans want to make sure he is well after a death hoax about the actor began circulating on social media.

Who is Ty Burrell?

Ty Burrell was born in Grants Pass, Oregon, on August 22, 1967.

The 56-year-old actor is widely known for his starring role as Phil Dunphy on ABC's Modern Family, which ran for 11 seasons from September 2009 to April 2020.

In 2011 and 2014, he earned the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series for his role in the sitcom.

Per his IMDb profile, Burrell has appeared in other films and TV series as well, such as The Incredible Hulk, Muppets Most Wanted, Rough Night, Boondoggle, Key and Peele, and The Skeleton Twins.

He has also worked as a voice actor for multiple movies and shows, such as Finding Dory, Duncanville, Storks, The Penguins of Madagascar, Mr. Peabody & Sherman, and Family Guy.

He has been married to his wife, Holly Burrell, since 2000.

The couple shares two kids, including daughters Frances and Greta Burrell.

As E! News reported, the pair adopted Frances in 2010 and Greta in 2012, though Greta's name had not yet been revealed at the time.
