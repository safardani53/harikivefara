KIM Kardashian has shocked fans with some new shirtless photos of boyfriend Pete Davidson.

 Kim, 42, shared a series of photos of herself and her beau, 28 on Instagram.

The post began with a pic of them smiling together and included several other glimpses into their relationship.

Pete is shirtless, with all of his tattoos on display, wearing a wool hat, grey sweat pants with his black Champion boxer-briefs sticking out of the top.

One of the photos shows Kim, 41,  casually lounging on the couch while shirtless Pete, sits in front of her on a table sticking out his tongue and holding up a peace sign.

NEW BODY ART

The photos showcased a new tattoo tributes to Kim on Pete's body.

The former Saturday Night Live funnyman has a new tattoo on his neck that refers to the very start of his relationship with the reality star.

On his shoulder, just below his neck, was the new tattoo, which simply said: "Jasmine and Aladdin."

The reference was to the now infamous Saturday Night Live skit last October, when Kim hosted the late night show, and played the famous Disney princess opposite Pete's Aladdin.

At the end of the skit, the pair leaned in for what would be their first kiss on the lips, before a genie popped up and sang A Whole New World.

Just a few weeks later, the two were confirmed to be a couple.

In April, Pete was spotted with a neck tattoo, not far from his new Aladdin ink, featuring all four of the reality star's children.

The tattoo simply says: "KNSCP."

The first initial stood for his famous girlfriend.

The order of the children's initials went from the oldest, North, to the youngest, Psalm. 

The TV star recently shared a snapshot of another one of the comic's ink pieces.

Kim posted a black-and-white close-up shot of Pete's tattoo.

In giant black letters, Pete gushed about how his "girl" happened to be a "lawyer."

The E! star had previously failed the bar exam numerous times before finally passing the test.
