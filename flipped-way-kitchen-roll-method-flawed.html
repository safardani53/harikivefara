PAPER towel holders have been a staple for millions of households across the world.

One influencer caused a stir when she showed off how she uses her metal stand.

Briona B (@Brihonor28) wowed TikTokers online when she turned the holder on its side before pushing it underneath a shelf.

The stand didn't fall over when Briona pulled sheets from the roll.

She then placed the kitchen stand behind her household essentials on a cupboard shelf.

But, TikTokers were skeptical as Briona didn’t show off her trick while the cupboard was closed.

Sarah Douglas said: “Show the cabinet closing.”

Meanwhile, another commented: “My cupboard door won’t close though.”

A third said: “But then you can’t close your cabinet lol.”

Briona hit back, claiming the cabinet closed after placing the holder inside.
