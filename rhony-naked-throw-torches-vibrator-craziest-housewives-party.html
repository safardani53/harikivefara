RHONY stars strip naked, throw fiery torches, and toss a vibrator in the chicken dish in craziest Ho

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 26, 2024

THE Real Housewives of New York stars stripped naked, threw lit tiki torches and left a vibrator on a chicken dish that was still sitting out the morning after the wild bash.

The insane party took place at Ramona Singer’s Hamptons house which had Sonja Morgan, 56, Tinsley Mortimer, 44, and newcomer Leah McSweeney, 37, skinny dipping in her backyard pool.

Leah - who joined the Bravo franchise in August 2019 - was the first to rip off her dress and dive into the water after a night of drinking vodka and champagne.

Despite previously opening up about her stint in rehab, Leah slowly eased her way back into alcohol since the season premiere - but her drunken behavior nearly destroyed Ramona's vacation home.

The RHONY star said as she removed her clothes: “I’m going to get naked, I’m going stir-crazy.

“There’s just only so much talking about yachts and déclassé that I can really handle before I’m like, ‘This is how I have fun. Watch and learn.’”

The fashion designer then convinced Tinsley to join her nude swim session and yelled “rip it off, b***h” before commanding her to “get in the pool.”

Leah then screamed: “This is a ‘f**k you’ to your mother, this is a ‘f**k you’ to high society. F**k you, Park Avenue!”

Tinsley was next in the pool and stripped down to her underwear before she dove in.

Sonja Morgan, 56, then revealed her bare butt as she removed her outfit and walked into the water holding a bottle of Prosecco.

“I feel young again and fun and careless,” she said as she hugged the topless ladies.

She continued to express her excitement: “Isn’t this the best party ever? We are the rebellious ones!”

Leah then went on a rant about the tiki torches before she and Sonja threw the fire-lit sticks into Romona’s grass.

“I hate these tiki torches! F**k you. They suck, they represent bulls**t!” Leah screamed.

Ramona - who left the women the previous night to go to another party - woke up to a disaster left in her kitchen and a very trashed backyard.

The reality star fired off as she observed the mess: “This is not cool! This is ridiculous, this is really bad. This is disgraceful! What were they doing?

“Throwing torches? This is torch fuel. A champagne bottle is in the pool? Are you kidding me? What were they doing? This is my home!”

As she discovered a dildo in a chicken dish left on the kitchen island, she continued:

“Really? Really? Really? Talk about no respect. Actually, I’m not mad. I’m not angry. I’m p****d! So disrespectful!”

Leah owned up to her part in the backyard mess: “I take responsibility for the tiki torches.

“I felt like I was in Game of Thrones, I got carried away. I tend to do things extra. Sometimes I drink extra. But I didn’t hurt anybody, I just threw tiki torches.”

Sonja also admitted to being at fault for the chaos but blamed the newbie: “Leah’s definitely a bad influence on me.”
