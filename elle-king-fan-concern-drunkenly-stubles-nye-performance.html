Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, September 11, 2024

ELLE King has sparked concern from fans after they watched her recent performance.

The country music singer performed on New Year's Eve Live: Nashville's Big Bash on Sunday night.

Elle, 34, sang her hit song Drunk (And I Don't Want To Go Home) that featured Miranda Lambert.

While singing, she walked down the catwalk stage and used a high-pitched voice singing the lyrics: "Baby, I'm drunk and I don't wanna go home" all while adding "Who does?" at the end.

As they were watching her perform one of her hit songs, fans took to X, formerly Twitter, to share their honest thoughts.

"#ElleKing on #CBSNashvilleNYE broadcast was absolutely cringe worthy. A fitting way to say goodbye to 2023," one wrote.

Another said: "Someone stop her this awful she is awful."

A third exclaimed: "Elle King is already drunk....I can't wait to see how smashed she is at Midnight!!"

"Elle King is drunk and doesn't want to go home. Hell, she's Janis Joplin type of wasted, although Joplin performed very well when wasted," claimed a fourth, referring to the singer's hit song that she sang.

Another person said: "I can't believe there isn't anyone who couldn't jump in and sing with #ElleKing. This is bad, cringy, just not good."

MORE ABOUT ELLE KING

Elle King - born Tanner Elle Schneider - was born on July 3, 1989 in Los Angeles, California.

Her parents are Rob Schneider and London King.

Rob is an actor, stand-up comedian, screenwriter, producer, and director who is well-known for starring in movies such as Grown Ups, The Hot Chick, The Animal, Big Stan, The Benchwarmers, and Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo.

Prior to Rob's acting career, he was known as a cast member and writer for Saturday Night Live from 1988 to 1994.

Elle's mother, London, is a former model and actress who is well-known for her role in A Better Way to Die, Raw Fish, and The Calling.

Rob and London tied the knot in 1988 and welcomed their daughter, Elle King a year later.

The couple later divorced in 1990.

HER NET WORTH

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Elle King has an estimated net worth of approximately $4million, as of April 2023.

Her main source of wealth is reportedly from the earnings from her music career.

In 2012, she released her debut EP, The Elle King EP, with the label RCA.

Her track, Playing For Keeps, became the theme song for VH1's Mob Wives Chicago series.

Three years later, King released her debut album, Love Stuff, and earned two Grammy Award nominations.

Her other two albums include Shake The Spirit (2018) and Come Get Your Wife (2023).

King has toured with artists such as Of Monsters And Men, Train, James Bay, The Chicks, and Heart.

She also earns income from brand endorsements and sponsorships.

HER RELATIONSHIP

Elle is engaged to Dan Tooker.

Dan is a tattoo artist who works with Riverside Tattoo Co.

Dan and Elle have a child together - a son named Lucky Levi Tooker.
