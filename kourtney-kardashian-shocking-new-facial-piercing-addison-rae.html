Kourtney Kardashian fans think she has a shocking new facial piercing in photo with friend Addison R

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

KOURTNEY Kardashian has shocked her fans with what appeared to be a new tongue piercing.

But the Kardashians star says there is an alternative explanation for her new look.

Kourtney and her BFF Addison Rae, who she's been friends with since 2020, posed together for a variety of photos to accompany a new Poosh.com article promoting how to make friends as an adult.

In the new photo set, the models smiled, laughed and made ducky faces at the camera.

Kourtney, 43, wore a black top that bared her belly paired with a full-length black skirt.

Addison, 22, countered her in a white spaghetti strap shirt, blue jeans, and pointed red heels.

However, it wasn't their sexy outfits that drew comments.

In the first photo, Kourtney and Addison stuck their tongues out, and the reality star revealed what looked like a dark piercing.

"Kourt, Tongue piercing?" one fan begged to know in the comments section.

Kourtney personally responded to the post, explaining: "It's red wine" while adding a laughing emoji.

If it wasn't a new piercing, some fans wished it was.

"It looks nice pierced," another person complimented. "Do it!!"

LIP SERVICE

It's not the first time Kourtney has surprised fans with an apparent new facial piercing.

Last year, she shared what she would look like with a lip piercing, thanks to the help of some Instagram filters.

In videos she posted online, Kourtney wore a black oversized T-shirt and posed with a diamond stud "piercing" on her bottom lip.

In another video, the mother-of-three had changed into a one-shoulder lace purple dress.

Clearly enjoying her grungy look, the Poosh founder placed the same stud-piercing filter over her face.

She pouted at the camera and turned her face from left to right to best show off the "piercing."

Days later, the accessory was back for Halloween, as Kourtney wore a goth-inspired outfit as she dropped her kids off at school.

BESTIES

Perhaps the only sight as shocking as the piercing was the coupling of Kourt and Addison.

The Kardashians star and Addison first became friends in 2020, when Addison appeared in a TikTok video with Kourt's son Mason.

The two soon became inseparable and they spent time together doing workout classes and taking exotic vacations.

In August of 2021, Addison Rae made her on-screen debut in the Netflix film, He's All That.

Throughout the movie, Kourtney Kardashian makes several cameo appearances as a brand executive working with Addison's character.

"We got very fortunate that it ended up working out and I'm so glad she got to join me on this," Kourtney said in an interview with Access at the time.

"I'm very, very thankful for her."

FALLING OUT

However, the women stopped spending as much time together around the same time things got serious between Kourtney and hubby Travis.

In an exclusive interview, a source close to Kourtney opened up to The Sun about the reason behind the pair's sudden rift.

"They got close for a while, but Kourtney is upset with Addison for a couple of reasons."

"Addison commented on Kourtney and Scott's relationship and other family drama on Watch What Happens Live when she should have just deflected the questions."

The source also said the duo's more than 20-year age difference affected the friendship.

"The fact of the matter is, Addison is closer to Kourtney's kids' ages than she is to Kourtney's age." 

But fans were pleasantly surprised to see Addison in photos at Travis' birthday party last year.
