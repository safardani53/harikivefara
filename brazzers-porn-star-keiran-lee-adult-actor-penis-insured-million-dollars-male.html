Keiran's career in porn began after his mates uploaded a naked photo of him, taken on a lads' holiday in Malia, onto a swingers website.

In August 2011, he was the main attraction on a Brazzers billboard on Sunset Boulevard - which has been described as a "landmark promotion" for a porn star.

As of July 2016, Keiran had appeared in 1,144 Brazzers movies.
