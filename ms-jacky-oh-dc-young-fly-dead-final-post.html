Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 4, 2024

WILD 'N Out star Ms Jacky Oh's devastating final post has emerged after the mom-of-three died at the age of 32.

The actress and long-time partner of comedian DC Young Fly died in Miami, Florida just days after giving a glimpse into her beautiful life with their kids.

BET Media Group confirmed Jacky's death on Thursday, sparking a social media storm mourning the loss of the Wild 'N Out cast member, whose real name is Jacklyn Smith.

According to her Instagram, life was blissful in the days leading up to her sudden passing.

In a post shared last week, Jacky celebrated putting her three little ones to bed at the same time.

"When u get all ur kids to sleep at once," she wrote while recording the beautiful babies that she shared with DC Young Fly.

An audio track playing over the footage was a man saying: "This part of my life is called happiness."

The mom went on to add the hashtags "happiness" and "peace."

Fans in the comment section initially gushed over the video until rumors circulated of her untimely death.

"Somebody please tell me it's not true," one person commented with a crying emoji.

Jacky was said to be undergoing a "mommy makeover" in Miami around the time of her death, according to TMZ.

One fan wrote: "Praying that surgery went well and that ur alright."

"I pray she's okay for her babies sake," another devastated follower said.

Jacky started dating DC Young Fly, whose real name is John Whitfield, in 2015, the same year he joined Wild 'N Out.

They have since welcomed three children, daughters Nova and Nala and son Prince.

While Jacky first broke into the entertainment scene with the MTV sketch comedy show, she eventually transitioned into being a businesswoman and mother.

Jacky worked as a realtor and also founded a beauty brand J Nova Collection that sold lip glosses, according to her Instagram.

She continued to act in films like House Party, which premiered in 2023 and featured stars like Kid Cudi.

BET, which produces Wild 'N Out, shared a heart-wrenching post mourning Jacky.

"We are deeply saddened by the passing of Jacklyn Oh, a talented Wild N' Out family member whose impact will be forever treasured and missed," a spokesperson wrote.

"Jacky Oh was a loving friend and beloved colleague of the Wild N' Out cast throughout five seasons.

"More importantly, she was a tremendous mother to three beautiful children.

"The BET Media Group extends our sincere condolences to the Smith family, DC Young Fly, B Simone, Nick Cannon, and all friends who loved and cared for Jacky Oh during this difficult time."
