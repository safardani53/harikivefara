Barmageddon fans ask where and when to watch Blake Sheltons hilarious game show Tue 14 Novem

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 6, 2024

Blake Shelton may have left his throne as ‘King of The Voice’ this year, but fans haven’t seen the last of him yet. Country legend Blake and his wife, Gwen Stefani, are raising the roof on season 2 of his show, Barmageddon. The show’s premiere welcomes viewers to Nashville, Tennesee. Kicking off the show’s festivities, Blake is on stage singing George Strait’s All My Ex’s Live in Texas.

  • When is Barmageddon on?
  • Meet the Barmageddon hosts

Where can you watch Barmageddon?

Barmageddon is a USA Network show. For USA viewers watching the show via cable, this is where you’ll find season 2 kicking off.

For those without cable, the show can be watched the day after it airs on Peacock.

More streaming options include Fubo, YouTube TV, Sling, and DirecTV.

When is Barmageddon on?

Barmageddon’s season 2 premiere kicked off on USA Network on Monday, November 13.

The show airs weekly on Mondays at 11/10c.

Fans can expect to see Blake Shelton and co enjoying their favorite bar games at his Nashville-based bar Ole Red. Live music sing-alongs are in store, as well as some surprise A-List arrivals.

Meet the Barmageddon hosts

Joining Blake Shelton in hosting Barmageddon are Carson Daly and Nikki Garcia.

Back for a second season, TV and radio host Carson is splitting his time between being at Blake’s bar, hosting The Voice, and appearing on The Today Show, by the looks of things.

The men are joined by someone who announcing must come very naturally to, WWE Hall of Famer Nikki Garcia. Blake describes her as his “sister from another mister.”

Famous faces appearing on the show right from episode 1 include Michelle Rodriguez, Boyz II Men, Kelly Clarkson, Nikki Glaser, and more celebrities.
