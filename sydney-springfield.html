Sydney Springfield | Career

Springfield played softball during high school. At the Flower Mound, she was a four-year standout. In her high school softball career, Sydney set her school home runs record with 25. 

After high school, Sydney enrolled at Lousiana State University, and right during her freshman year, she played 57 games in total. Her freshman year went by with success, and in her sophomore year, she played 24 games. 

As for her junior year, Sydney transferred to Oklahoma State University as the Cowgirl coaching staff’s first pinch hitter. There, she played 15 games, including 12 at first base.

By the end of the year, she surpassed the record of being the only Cowgirl with more than three base knocks as a pinch hitter.
