Anna Cardwell appeared happy in final post as Mama June's daughter interacted with fans weeks before

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

MAMA June's daughter Anna Cardwell has died, but she appeared happy in her final post as she interacted with fans just weeks earlier.

The star was diagnosed with Stage 4 adrenal carcinoma in January and her final post to her fans amid her battle was in November.

In Anna's post, she a health update as she un-bagged pickle-themed candy.

Although she was struggling with her health at the time, she appeared energetic in the video.

Anna captioned her final post: "Y’all go try these pickles out they are amazing..."

Several fans poured into Anna's Instagram comments to share their thoughts and prayers.

"Rest in peace Ana. May Jesus receive you and comfort your family's hearts," one person shared.

Another added: "Rest in peace, Anna. You gave one hell of a fight you were so pure."

June's eldest daughter died late last night and the mom-of-four confirmed the heartbreaking news via social media.

IN MEMORY

On December 9, June revealed the heartbreaking news that Anna had lost her cancer battle.

Most read in Entertainment

In July, the star, 44, shared that her daughter's diagnosis was terminal.

"For me, it's an emotional rollercoaster sometimes," she told Entertainment Tonight.

"Mentally, it's always on my mind. We know it's terminal."

June added: "She's Stage 4. She's not gonna go into remission. We've all accepted that, so I just tell people one day at a time 'cause you never know."

Upon her death, Mama June's husband Justin Stroud posted a photo on his Instagram Feed to announce the death of his step-daughter.

The picture showed the whole family sitting on what appeared to be the set of the TLC show.

He captioned the post: "Please keep our family in your prayers as we lost one of our angels last night. She passed away last night in our home peacefully with the people that was important to her.

"Please be respectful and we will keep y’all updated with everything. We love you Anna Banana."

Mama June used the same photo for her memorial post which read: "With the breaking heart, we are announcing that [Anna] is no longer with us. She passed away in my home last night peacefully at 11:12 PM.

"She gave one hell of a fight for 10 months she passed away with her family around her like she won’t and we will be updating y’all with more information as we get it today.

"We love y’all and continue prayers and thoughts for our family during this difficult time."

HEARTBREAKING LOSS

Anna's famous sister Alana Thompson, also known as "Honey Boo Boo," took to her Instagram to share a family photo.

She captioned the post she re-shared to her Story: "Fly high for me beautiful."

In the actual caption, Alana wrote: "This is one post I wish I didn’t have to make.

"Last night we all surrounded Anna with love and let her know it was okay to go.

"Unfortunately around 11 PM Anna took her last breathe."

Alana, 18, continued: "Anna was in so much pain last night but now as a family we all know she is at peace now.

"I really don’t know what to say as my heart is completely broken."

After sharing her final sentiments, the star concluded: "We will always love you Anna.

"You hit me hard with his one Anna but I know you're in a better place now and pain-free forever!"

Anna is survived by her eldest daughters - Kaitlyn, 10, and Kylee, seven - the latter who she shared with ex-husband Michael.

Besides her mother, Anna is also survived by three sisters - Alana, Lauryn "Pumpkin" Efird, 23, and Jessica "Chubbs" Shannon, 26, who fans have watched on the family's multiple reality shows.
