Big Time Rush Can't Get Enough 2023 Tour: How can I buy tickets?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 15, 2024

BIG Time Rush is known as a boy band that formed in 2009 and was the subject of a Nickelodeon show of the same name that ran from 2009 to 2013.

After their 2022 Forever Tour success, the band is embarking on yet another Summer 2023 cross-country tour.

How can I buy tickets for the 2023 Big Time Rush Can't Get Enough Tour?

Tickets for Big Time Rush's tour go on sale on February 10, 2023, and can be purchased through Ticketmaster.

The Can't Get Enough Tour announcement came after their special appearance on NBC's Today on February 6, 2023.

Big Time Rush exclusively premiered their song Can't Get Enough before announcing the tour news.

Tickets range from $20 to $500 plus.

If you're part of the Big Time Rush fan club, members can purchase presale tickets starting at 10am February 7 until February 9th at 10pm.

Citi card members all have the opportunity to purchase tour tickets starting February 8th at 10am until the following day at 10pm.

Joining BTR on stage will be singers MAX and Jax.

Where is Big Time Rush playing?

From June 2023 to August 2023, BTR will be playing over 35 shows.

Who are the members of Big Time Rush?

Big Time Rush is composed of four members including Kendall Schmidt, James Maslow, Logan Henderson, and Carlos PenaVega.

All four were originally signed with Nick Records in 2009 before transferring to Columbia Records.

Following the end of the BTR tv show, the boys were on an indefinite hiatus to work on their solo careers but remained close.

While Logan, Carlos, and James went on to release solo material, Kendall rejoined the duo Heffron Drive, alongside Dustin Belt, which he had originally formed in 2008.
