Cardi B Shows Off Kulture's Custom Diamond Bracelet

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, July 11, 2024

Where Kulture Kiari‘s mama’s got rings, she’s got bracelets.

Cardi B took to Instagram Wednesday to show off her 7-month-old daughter’s new bling: a custom diamond bracelet from Shyne Jewelers, which was designed to spell out Kulture’s name in cursive.

“KULTURE @shynejewelers,” the rapper, 26, wrote alongside her photo, while the jewelry company shared a video showcasing the bracelet’s details set to Cardi B‘s fitting track “Money.”

“Thank you @iamcardib for letting us make Kulture this masterpiece!” the caption on the clip read.

Many fans were quick to fawn over the baby girl’s new accessory in Cardi B’s comments section, while others pointed out the cost with remarks like, “This baby got my whole college tuition on her wrist and the age of not even 1 must be nice.”

“Damn shorty got my mortgage on her wrist smh,” another joked.

A third fan lamented, “Lol that bracelet for not even a one year old could probably pay for my rent this year haha.”

Even before Cardi B and husband Offset welcomed their first child together on July 10, the “I Like It” rapper was stocking up on high-ticket items while she was still pregnant.

Cardi B added a bevy of expensive items to her baby registry, TMZ reported in June following her lavish baby shower in Atlanta.

Later, she showed off items like Kulture’s gold crib and, more recently, shared a photo of her daughter sporting a white-and-gold patterned designer snowsuit while on a private flight with her mama.

The hooded puffer ensemble the infant wore, by Versace, retails for $830 on the designer’s website. The onesie is also available on farfetch.com for a bit cheaper, at $594.

That doesn’t mean the Grammy winner doesn’t know how to shop a bargain or understand the necessity of items that are a bit more practical when it comes to babies.

In an exclusive interview in Atlanta ahead of the Super Bowl earlier this month, Cardi B told PEOPLE that her baby girl may rock high-end brands on occasion, but her day-to-day wardrobe is much different.

“She has so much name brands, and she just throws up in all of them,” joked the rapper of little Kulture. “She always ends up [wearing] Target pajamas.”

“I look at every video and pictures and she got Target pajamas because by the time I take a picture … very pretty, expensive clothes, she throws up on it, she s—s on it, she drools all over it,” added the mother of one. “Things never go as you plan.”
