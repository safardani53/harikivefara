Im a 56 gym girl who weighs 147 lbs - people say I have an amazing figure when I pose

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, March 5, 2024

A FITNESS enthusiast has revealed her measurements in a candid TikTok post.

She said at five-foot-six she weighs 147 pounds and gave a demonstration of what her figure looks like by posing in the gym.

Elina (@elimariagld) revealed her photo evidence of her stature, which she largely built in the gym.

"So apparently we are showing our weight and height to show how it looks on different bodies," she said in the video.

She showed herself in various athletic wear while posing in the gym.

Elina showed off her toned physique in a bikini mirror selfie.

The gym girl also included herself on a night out, sporting leather leggings and a crop top.

"You will always weigh the most in the evenings," she added in the caption.

The video came as part of a trend of fitness fans sharing their personal journeys on social media.

Many are coming out to share their measurements to dispel myths about weight.

The gym girls are detailing their weight and showing off their hard-earned curves.

The video struck a chord with viewers who shared their thoughts in the comment section.

"You have an amazing figure," one wrote.

"Girl!" another admirer exclaimed.

Elina shared the reason for revealing her measurements on social media.

"#SelfLove," she added.
