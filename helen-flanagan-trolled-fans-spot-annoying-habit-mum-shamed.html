Helen Flanagan cruelly trolled again as fans spot annoying habit in latest snaps

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 21, 2024

CORONATION Street beauty Helen Flanagan has found herself trolled over an "annoying habit".

The 33-year-old came under fire from some followers for always having her "cleavage on show".

On Instagram, Helen shared a video experimenting with various products. She captioned the post: "Festive day make up. kindness will always make you pretty."

In the video, the Coronation Street actress wore a Christmas pyjama set, leaving all the buttons undone down to just below her chest, revealing full cleavage.

One user commented: "Why do you need to have your zip down to advertise face cream."

Another added: "Wonder how many more likes the cleavage gets."

A third penned: "Cant say i noticed the makeup bit."

Some fans defended Helen with one writing: "The hate on this page for Helen is just horrible!

"Why are you people even following her as you clearly don’t like what she is doing! Just unfollow her! She is a beautiful lady and a wonderful mum!"

Another added: "All the women tearing this girl to shreds in the comments you should be ashamed of yourselves and bringing her kids into it!!! Go have a long hard look at yourselves!!!!"
