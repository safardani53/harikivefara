A Look at His Mom [2023 Update]

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, June 2, 2024

The American football player Mike Williams never forgets to keep an appreciation post for his mother on Mother’s Day. Francina Palmer, his mother, is truly a fantastic mother who is her son’s biggest supporter and inspiration, and she rightly deserves the highest level of love from her kids.

One of the top picks of the 2017 draft, Mike has been playing as a wide receiver for the Los Angeles Chargers of the NFL. 

Having played in just six league seasons, the South Carolina-born athlete has amassed a whooping net worth of about $5 million as of 2023.

Francina tried raising her kids, including Mike, alone after her husband’s death. She was doing great, but she did not want them to feel the absence of their dad, so she remarried.

Mike understands it all and appreciates his mom for whatever she did to keep his dream alive despite all the hardships she endured.

This article is about Mike’s brave mother and the sacrifices she made to help Mike reach where he is today. Please read till the end to know more about her and Mike.

Francina Palmer

Francina Palmer was born to her mother, Ruthie Williams, in South Carolina, United States. Although there is no detailed information about her dad and siblings, Francina has spent her entire life in South Carolina.

In her youth, she met Mike’s dad and fell in love. After dating for a while, their relationship got serious, and she gave birth to her two babies, a son and a daughter. 

Unfortunately, her first love passed away in a car accident in 1997, which left young Francina alone with her children. Mike was just three at the time of his dad’s death. 

Mike’s biological father’s name was Michael, and people now remember him as the big Mike. The football player has a tattoo on his foot as a tribute to his late dad.

Francina married her new husband, Willie Palmer, on March 23, 2000. Willie is originally from New York. The couple later gave birth to a son, and they live in Vance, South California. 

Francina’s only daughter, I’Asia, is in a relationship with De’Riante Jenkins, a professional basketball player. She has been living with him in Italy with their two kids, TyMir and Taylor.

A Brief Bio On Mike Williams

Michael K. Williams, better known as Mike Williams, was born to his parents, Mike and Francina, on October 4, 1997, in Vance, South Carolina, United States. As stated above, he was named after his dad.

Young Mike grew up with his two siblings in Vance and attended Lake Marion High School in Santee, nearby his locality.

Gifted athletically and academically, he graduated high school as a four-star recruit with good grades.

He later enrolled in Clemson University in South Carolina and played college football for the Clemson Tigers almost until 2017.

The Los Angeles Chargers of the NFL chose Williams as the seventh overall pick in the 2017 NFL Draft. With that selection, he became the second wide receiver to get drafted in that year’s draft after Corey Davis.

Corey Davis is another NFL star of a proud mother Michelle Davis.

The club signed a four-year contract worth nearly $20 million with Mike on May 11, 2017.

Although Mike missed his entire training camp and some initial regular season matches, he has been doing pretty well for them as of late. As a result, the Chargers have decided to keep him until the end of the 2025 season.

Mike signed a new three-year deal worth $60 million with them on March 8, 2022.
