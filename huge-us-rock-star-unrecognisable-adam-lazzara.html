Huge US rock star unrecognisable 18 years after smash hit album, feud and bandmates quitting in bitt

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, June 5, 2024

A HUGE US rock star looks unrecognisable 18 years after his smash hit album and bitter bandmate row.

Adam Lazzara shot to fame with his New York rock band Taking Back Sunday.

The now 42-year-old is the lead vocalist of the group, which is still together.

Adam was known for his long brunette locks but these days he sports a much lighter complexion.

In a recent post on Instagram, the singer showed off his bleached blonde hair.

Lazzara also still has his tightly trimmed beard.

Taking Back Sunday was formed in 1999 by Eddie Reyes and bassist Jesse Lacey.

However, Jesse left the band while they were still recording their first six track EP to form a new group called Brand New.

According to reports, Jesse left the band after discovering his fellow bandmate John Nolan had allegedly hooked up with a woman he was seeing at the time.

It led to a public feud between the bandmates and Jesse promptly departing the group.

Adam was then brought in to replace the star as the band's new bassist before becoming lead vocalist.

Taking Back Sunday have released eight studio albums to date, with their latest, 152, coming out earlier this year.
