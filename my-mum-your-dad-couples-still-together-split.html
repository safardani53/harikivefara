My Mum Your Dad couples who are still together - and who split after the show

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, February 1, 2024

MY Mum Your Dad hit our screens last week and has had viewers hooked every week night ever since.

The ITV show, hosted by Davina McCall, was billed as the Love Island for middle-aged single parents - however the format is actually very different.

Unlike the brutal recouplings and racy games in the Mallorca villa, which are there to tempt the contestants into cheating, the parents are given the chance to get to know each other and go on dates.

But little do they know that their children are watching their every move in a surveillance room called 'The Bunker'.

The youngsters have been able to guide them during their time in the mansion, but have also been cringingl as they watched their mums and dads lock lips.

How does the show work?

The adults, who were nominated by their kids to enter the retreat to find love, all live together in a big country house.

Caroline, Elliot, Clayton, Monique, Natalie, Paul, Rodger and Sharon entered on day one, followed by Janey on day two.

Martin H surprised them with his arrival on day three, followed by Martin M and Tolullah the following day.

Host Davina visits the parents to discuss their progress, in what feels like a big group therapy session, where they open up about their past relationships and where its gone wrong, or in some cases, where they've lost loved ones.

Who is coupled up with who?

Some of the cast developed good connections and there were a handful that were going strong at the end:

However, Martin M, Martin H, Tolullah and Monique found themselves in a "love square".

Martin M and Monique clicked while Martin H and Tolullah also hit it off - but they then swapped partners.

Who has already left?

Caroline and Clayton were the first pair to leave My Mum Your Dad during Tuesday night's (19th September) episode.

The pair left the house as pals with no romantic connection.

Some viewers hit out after they shared their opinion that ‘the wrong parents’ were home during the first dumping.

When will the final air?

Fans were gutted to know that My Mum Your Dad only consisted of 10 episodes.

The show came to an end on Friday September 22.
