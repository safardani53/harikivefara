Hosting live from Wembley arena in London, Alesha and Jason kicked off a huge party looking back at the biggest moments of the past year.

There were performances ranging from pop group Westlife and Katherine Jenkins to Joel Corry and Adam Lambert.

And appearances from the likes of Oti Mabuse, Tom Grennan, The Sugababes, Mimi Webb, RAYE and Cirque du Soleil, while those people who have used The National Lottery funding to make a difference to others' lives were also celebrated.

Alesha has been the Queen of Christmas TV this year appearing for more than seven hours during the season, in five major shows across the BBC and ITV.

She is a panellist on ITV's one-off Christmas Eve special Britain Get Singing, where stars from programmes such as The Chase and Coronation Street compete as choirs.

She also judged the festive Britain's Got Talent spin-off Ultimate Magician alongside Amanda Holden, David Walliams and Penn Jillette.

Mo Gilligan invited Alesha on to his new BBC game show That's My Jam and then the mum-of-two also starred on Michael McIntyre's Christmas Wheel on December 25.

Tonight wraps up her final show of the year.
