Michael Kors 90 ballet flats crowned the UK's favourite shoe - beating competition from Manolos

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 29, 2024

WHEN it comes to the nation's favourite designer shoe, most clued-up fashion fans would assume that a coveted pair of Manolo Blahnik heels or the A-List approved Louboutins would take the top spot.

But if this surprising new research is anything to go by, both these luxury shoe designers have been beaten to the punch by an understated pair of Michael Kors ballet flats...

  • Michael Kors Fulton Leather Pumps, £90 from Selfridges - buy now

According to the Daily Mail, researchers looking into the UK's most popular designer shoes found that Michael Kors' classic leather Fulton flats receive the most Instagram hashtags in a single week.

Racking up a staggering 28,000 posts the average seven day period, the simple shoes stormed ahead in the race with their nearest rival - the Valentino Rockstuds - which only attract a cool 11,000.

Collaborating with Chester Racecourse to investigate the nation's favourite luxury buys, not only were the Fulton flats the most popular shoes by far... they were also the cheapest.

But seeing as they're also available in a whole host of colours and patterns, we personally think the Fulton flat's £90 price tag has something to do with their popularity.

After all, who wouldn't be tempted by a reasonably priced classic leather pump over a bank-breaking statement heel?

Especially the Valentino Rockstud runner-ups cost up to £1,050 for a super sparkly pair.

What's more, other super expensive designs which made the top 10 include the £775 crystal Manolos worn by Carrie Bradshaw in the SATC movie.

Not to mention the Chloe Susanna boots which will set fashion fans back £850... but luckily, here's how you can snap up a dupe for just £28.

Unsurprisingly, Christian Louboutin's £495 signature Pigalle stilettos also made an appearance on the list as well as the £540 Gucci Horsebit loafters which are beloved by fashion bloggers.
