KHLOE Kardashian has fired back at fans taking her to task for flaunting her wealth while preaching humility and kindness.

Khloe took to her Instagram Stories on Saturday with a response to recent criticism.

The 39-year-old posted a simple black-and-white quote without additional commentary.

"Raise your children to be impressed by kindness, loyalty and humility, not just wealth, possessions and power," the quote read.

Critics had slammed Khloe and her sisters for spending so much money over the holiday season.

"Does the Kardashians excessive spending make you sick?" one irate fan asked on Reddit.

Implant rippling usually refers to the folds and wrinkles on the implant that are visible on the skin.

This happens for women who have had boob jobs with saline or silicone breast implants and usually develops on the outer perimeter of the breasts.
