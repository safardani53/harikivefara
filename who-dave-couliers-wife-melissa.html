Who is Dave Coulier's wife Melissa?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 23, 2024

DAVE Coulier rose to fame in the late 1980s as Joey Gladstone on ABC's sitcom Full House.

Since then, he has gotten married twice, and now fans want to know more about his wife, Melissa Bring.

Who is Dave Coulier's wife Melissa?

Following Coulier's divorce from Jayne Modean in 1992, he tied the knot with Melissa Bring in 2014.

Like her husband, she is known for her career in the entertainment industry and has worked on a number of projects over the years including, The Arrangement, Can't Get Arrested, and the upcoming documentary, Stripped: Montana.

She is also known for her photography and is often seen posting her pictures on Instagram to 54,000 followers.

Do Dave and Melissa have any children together?

Despite being married for almost a decade, Bring and Coulier do not have any children together.

The Fuller House alum previously welcomed a son, Luc, 31, during his previous marriage to Modean.
