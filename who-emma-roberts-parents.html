Who are Emma Roberts' parents?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

EMMA Roberts made a name for herself on the Nickelodeon show Unfabulous, which ran from 2004 to 2007.

At the time, Emma was only 13 years old, but she was used to the spotlight thanks to her famous parents.

Who are Emma Roberts' parents?

Emma Roberts was born on February 10, 1991, in Rhinebeck Village, Rhinebeck, New York, to Eric Roberts and Kelly Cunningham.

Like Emma, Eric and Kelly are also actors in the entertainment industry.

Eric is known for his roles in Runaway Train (1985), The Dark Knight (2008), The Expendables (2010), and Inherent Vice (2014), while Kelly is known for her roles in Billy Club (2013), Badsville (2017), Silent Love Stories-II (2018), and Legal ThreaT (2020).

Despite having a child together, Eric and Kelly never married, however, they were in a relationship from 1988 to 1991.

Eric went on to marry Eliza Roberts in 1992 while Kelly married and divorced bassist Kelly Nickels.

Over the years, Emma has kept her relationship with her parents out of the spotlight, but fans got a glimpse in February 2023, when she called out her mom for posting a photo of her son's face without her permission.

"When your mom posts your son's face without asking but you love them both so whatever ðŸ’–," Emma wrote on her Instagram Story after her mom posted a picture of her son Rhodes' face "without asking," according to PEOPLE.

Prior to this, Kelly accidentally "spilled the beans" about her pregnancy, PEOPLE notes.

Emma welcomed son Rhodes in 2020 with actor Garrett Hedlund.

Does Emma Roberts have siblings?

Emma is Eric's only biological child, however, she does have two step-siblings from Eliza's previous relationship with film producer James Simons.

Prior to her marriage to Eric, she welcomed Keaton Simons, who is now known as a recording and performing artist, and Morgan Simons, who is now known as a casting director.

Emma also has a half-sister, Grace Nickels, from her mother's previous marriage.

What is Emma Roberts' net worth?

Due to Emma's successful career as a model, actress, and singer, she has been able to amass a fortune over the years.

As of April 2023, she has an estimated net worth of $25million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

She is one of the wealthiest in the Roberts family, but her aunt, Julia Roberts, arguably has the highest worth, with an estimated fortune of $250million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.
