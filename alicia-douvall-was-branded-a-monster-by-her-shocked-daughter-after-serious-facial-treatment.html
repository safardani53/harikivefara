Alicia Douvall was branded a 'MONSTER' by her shocked daughter after serious facial treatment

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, February 20, 2024

COSMETIC surgery addict Alicia Douvall was branded a MONSTER by her own daughter after a hardcore facial treatment left her looking like a 'burns victim'.

Simon Cowell's ex has forked out more than £1.5million on 350 cosmetic procedures, spanning the past 20 years.

This includes 16 boob jobs and having her nose done SEVEN times.

Yet it was a full dermabrasion procedure a few years ago, which removed the top layer of skin from model Alicia's face, which terrified Georgia, who was then just a little girl.

The youngster was staying with family while her mum visited the clinic and after seeing her emerge with a red raw face, said: "I didn't recognise her.

"I didn't even known it was a person, never mind my mum.

"I remember saying to my gran 'It's a monster'."

During her youth Georgia, now 21, spent much of her time seeing her mum on the treatment table or recovering from operations in bandages.

The latest of Alicia's facial pep-up was implants, after the mum-of-two craved the cushion-cheek look of Angelina Jolie.

Explaining they hadn't gone to plan, Alicia told the Mail Online: "Instead, I looked like an alien. Even Georgia, my daughter, said: “Oh my God, you have to get that fixed” — and she never tells me I look a mess."

She added: "‘I was so selfish,’ Alicia says now. ‘Georgia has always been more like the mother. She still looks after me.’

Alicia’s own quest for perfection using plastic surgery was fuelled by her body dysmorphia, an anxiety-related condition which causes the sufferer to have a distorted view of their appearance.

She’s been open about the repercussions of having multiple procedures, including the possibility of her bottom lip collapsing AND a boob implant that almost killed her.

Talking of her numerous procedures she added to Mail Online: "It was always boobs and bum. I thought if I looked like a porn star, then men would find me attractive."

Her main surgery threat now is her leaky silicon bump implants - which could kill her.

Yet Alicia told ITV lunchtime show Loose Women she would need more surgery to correct the fault, something she isn't willing to do.

The Loose Women panellists couldn’t hide their shock with Gloria Hunniford telling Alicia: “At least you’d be alive…” when Alicia said she was in two minds about surgery.

Alicia added: “I don’t want surgery ever again – there are so many complications."
