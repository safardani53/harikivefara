Topless! Skiing! Probably Freezing! Looking to Rile Up Instagram!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, April 18, 2024

We hope Chelsea Handler didn’t catch a cold Friday.

Our concern stems from the fact that she was out skiing, atop a snowy mountain and looking off in the distance, ostensibly looking for her lost puppy.

She was clearly looking for controversy, however, and not just her beloved canine. Her search was likely not fans’ biggest takeaway from this image …

If you hadn’t noticed, Chelsea was also topless.

“I lost chunk on the mountain,” she teased.

She also lost the racy pic from her feed.

The latest Chelsea Handler nude offering was taken down real fast, after which she responded on Twitter, joking, “Finding chunk. Instagram had him!”

This woman has never been shy, that’s for sure.

Two months ago, the 39-year-old comedienne slammed Instagram for taking down a picture of herself riding a horse topless and mocking Vladimir Putin.

She was not happy, and let them know it.

“Taking this down is sexist,” she said.

“I have every right to prove I have a better body than Putin,” she tweeted. “If a man posts a photo of his nipples, it’s ok, but not a woman? Are we in 1825?”

No. No we are not. And if Kylie and Kendall Jenner have taught us anything, it’s that going topless on Instagram is pretty darn common these days.

See Miley Cyrus topless … this weekend!

Who hasn’t posed sans tops and shirts in this day and age? Yet Chelsea throws one little nip in there and all hell breaks loose over at IG HQ, though.

SMH, people. SMH.
