Who Is Lacey Evans Dating Currently? Who Is The Father Of Her Kid?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, July 28, 2024

Macey Estella-Kadlec, who is professionally known as Lacey Evans, is a famous American professional wrestler currently signed to WWE. The professional wrestler is currently on maternity leave since February 2021.

It was her personal life as well, which dragged her to the limelight. Do you know she is dating one of the biggest stars of WWE? Who is the legendary figure? Know everything below:

Is Lacey Evans Dating Ric Flair?

Due to the beautiful chemistry shared on the television screen, many fans reported that she and Ric Flair are dating in real life too. However, confirmation is yet to come. Many tabloids have speculated that they are a real-life couple too.

Just a couple of weeks before Evans and Flair started dating on-screen on WWE; many gossip mongers confirmed that she took her Instagram to continue her storyline even before it started.

CAPTION: Lacey Evans is rumored to be dating Ric Flair SOURCE: Instagram @ laceyevanswwe

Evans even posted a picture of her on Instagram, which seems flirtatious with Flair. As per her recent Twitter posts, it seems like they have planned to tie the knot.

Who Is The Father Of Her Child?

It is no secret that Evans is currently pregnant. The news spread like wildfire when the professional wrestler revealed that she was pregnant after her romance with Flair flashed on the media.

Just a couple of weeks after the relationship announcement, she also confirmed her pregnancy. She announced her pregnancy in front of Riki Johnson’s wife Charlotte, who was also surprised at the moment.

The source has confirmed that the pregnancy is not fake. Many sources suspected if Flair is the father of her kid.

But some tabloids claimed that her pregnancy is not related to the professional wrestler. Evans was set to face Asuka at the Elimination Chamber, but the match got canceled. 

She is hoping to have a baby boy.

As per Wrestling-Edge, her husband Alfonso is the father of her to-be-born kid.

Is She Still Married?

Evans’s personal life is quite confusing. While there are many of her romance rumors with Flair, many sources have confirmed that she is still married to her husband, Alfonso Estrella-Kadlec.

Evans has also posted numerous pictures with Flair on her Instagram, indicting flirting with him. But has not uploaded recent pictures of her husband.
