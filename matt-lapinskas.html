Matt Lapinskas’s Age & Early Life

Matthew William Lapinskas was born on February 27, 1989, in Surrey, England, the United Kingdom. He is the son of John and Teresa Lapinskas and is of Canadian and Lithuanian descent. Jonathan Kieran Lapinskas, his elder brother, was born in 1987. He attended Brooklands College to pursue a BTEC National Diploma in Performing Arts and became a member of their theatrical company, where he received many accolades for his performance in The Opposite of People.

Matt then went on to study acting at Italia Conti in London from 2007 to 2010, graduating with a BA(HONS) in 2010. He sings and plays the guitar. He is also an ardent football lover who promotes Manchester United, and plays frequent philanthropic football games for ‘Once Upon a Smile’.

Matt Lapinskas Height & Weight

Matt Lapinskas is 5 feet 10 inches (1.78m) tall and weighs 63 kg. His hair is dark brown, and his eyes are hazel brown.

Matt Lapinskas’s Net Worth

How much is Matt Lapinskas’s net worth? He is estimated to have a net worth of $2 million as of August 2023. His excellent profession as an actor is obviously his primary source of money. 

Career

Matt Lapinskas appeared as an understudy in Stephen Surjik’s 2007 film I Want Candy, and in 2011, he was cast as Anthony Moon in the BBC soap opera EastEnders. On May 9, 2012, Daniel Kilkelly of Digital Spy revealed that Lapinskas will be leaving EastEnders and that his character would be disappearing from the show in the following weeks. In an interview with Inside Soap, Lapinskas said that EastEnders was his first job outside of show business and that he learned a lot from the people he worked with.

He was revealed as one of 12 VIPs competing in the eighth season of Dancing on Ice in December 2012. He also toured the country as ‘Pharoah’ in Bill Kenwright’s production of Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat. During this period, he met his current love Camilla Rowland.

Matt Lapinskas Wife & Marriage

Who is Matt Lapinskas’s wife? Matt Lapinskas was previously linked to his former EastEnders co-star Shona McGarty, who portrays Whitney Dean. They met on his first day on-site and started dating two months later. It was described as “affection at first sight” by Lapinskas.

The couple divorced in February 2013, after allegations that Lapinskas was having extramarital affairs with Dancing on Ice partner Brianne Delcourt. He is no longer with Delcourt, and after a tumultuous split, she went to The Mirror and accused him of cheating on her with his current girlfriend, an on-screen persona and model Camilla Rowland, but inside sources confirm that the pair had formally separated by then. He is potentially single at the moment.
