Harry Styles set for awkward 'showdown' with Olivia Wilde's ex Jason Sudeikis, after she flew to Lon

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, February 15, 2024

WHEN news of their relationship emerged early this year, Harry Styles and Olivia Wilde’s romance looked set to be overshadowed by an awkward row with her ex Jason Sudeikis.

But it looks as though the A-list pairing, who met when film-maker Olivia hired pop singer Harry to be the leading man in her new movie, are turning up the heat.

I’m told Olivia has now flown to London to spend time with her hunky younger love, with the glamorous duo expected to spend time together at his home after he previously flew to London to start work on his next filming project.

'THEY DON'T WANT TO BE APART FOR LONG'

After snaps of her appearing to take belongings from her former family home in Los Angeles to Harry’s place emerged last week, their latest move is a clear sign that things are ­getting serious fast.

A source said: “They clearly don’t want to be apart for long, because Olivia has decided to come to ­London and is going to be staying with him.

“Obviously things are still early days — they’ve only been properly dating since last November, but things have been moving fast since they went public together at his manager’s ­wedding.

“The latest move may also mean they all end up seeing each other, as Jason is in London filming his Apple TV sitcom Ted Lasso and ­currently has his kids by Olivia staying with him, so no doubt there is going to be some crossover among them.

“It’s certainly going to be interesting given the way things have been so far — but nothing is getting in the way for Olivia and Harry just now.”

I previously told how a ­dispute over when Olivia and Jason fully split had sparked serious rows between them — after he suggested she had begun seeing Harry before their relationship ended, something those close to her have always denied.

And last week it emerged that ­producers at Harry’s new film are looking at possibly using studio space at the same complex as Jason’s show, ­setting up the prospect of an impromptu and uncomfortable face-to-face meeting for the two men.

But after The Sun revealed Hollywood frontman Jason is now also ready to move on, having quickly grown close to former Page 3 girl ­Keeley Hazell, there may well be plenty for them all to talk about if a get-together does take place.

One thing’s for sure — that’d be one good-looking foursome, however awkward the chat might be.

It's a Lottie hard work

MODELS are always insisting their job is a lot harder than it seems, but  it looks like Lottie Moss is living the dream.

The sister of supermodel Kate recently moved to Los Angeles and this week was taken to Palm Springs for a photoshoot with clothing firm Pretty Little Thing.

It must have been taxing work posing in a leopard-print bikini in a sun-kissed pool while sipping on a margarita.

Along with modelling for fashion  brands, Lottie recently signed up to Glow –  described as an “upmarket”  OnlyFans – where fans pay just under £4 to access never-before-seen shots of her.

Lottie started flogging snaps on the site earlier this month alongside ­Justin Bieber’s ex-girlfriend Sahara Ray, who was in Palm Springs with her.

I’d rather spend my cash on a pint when the pubs finally open, but I’ll bear it in mind.

Scarlett: I want funeral to be like a sad karaoke

SCARLETT Moffatt has been using all this time stuck at home to plan her own funeral.

The former Gogglebox favourite and TV presenter admits  it is morbid but insists she is very picky about how the ceremony should be.

 She said: “I’ve already planned out my funeral. Mine’s going to be like a karaoke, I want that many songs. I even know what velvet I want  inside my coffin.

“I want everyone to be really sad. I don’t want it to be joyous, I want ­people to go, ‘God, we’re really going to miss her.’ 

“So I want projectors either side of my coffin playing old episodes of Gogglebox and Saturday Night Takeaway, but just of me laughing in slow motion and looking really joyous so that everyone can go, ‘Look how happy she was’.”

Justin's £400k blow

JUSTIN and Hailey ­Bieber have sold their ­Beverly Hills mansion – for a £388,000 loss.

The couple only bought the luxurious property  two years ago but  have already flogged it for far less than they paid.

They splashed out £6.06million for the pad  in 2019  but put it back on the market in October and it recently shifted for £5.66million.

They offloaded it when they bought another swanky residence for a whopping £18.5million.

Justin and Hailey are clearly bringing in the big bucks – just not as ­property speculators.

Gary set for solo bid again

WITH all their bickering, there’s no chance of  a ­spandau Ballet reunion any time soon, so Gary Kemp is taking things into his own hands.

I can reveal the star is going solo with an album due in June and the first single out next month.

He has been snapped up by Columbia Records – home to Adele, Beyonce and Bob Dylan –  despite his first solo record,  Little Bruises, 25 years ago failing to make much of  an impact. Gary has teamed  up with Pink Floyd’s touring bassist Guy Pratt, who is a member of Floyd drummer Nick Mason’s  Saucerful Of Secrets band.

Gary said: “I did a record, all remote.

“Well, a lot of it was remote, apart from in  the ­summer when we could meet up.

“It’s finished and coming out in a few months. I roped Guy in.”

The news comes after  many years of arguments with former ­Spandau singer Tony Hadley, which forced Gary  to admit last year: “There are  no plans for Spandau going into 2020.”

I’ve got my fingers crossed his solo efforts fare better this time around.

Grim for North

IF you think you’ve had a bad week, spare a thought for ­jordan North.

The Radio 1 DJ and I’m A Celeb finalist ­accidentally binned his phone, lost his wallet and had a letter from the bailiffs.

He said: “It was my birthday on Sunday and I think I’d had a few too many and decided to do the recycling.

“I couldn’t find my phone all Sunday night. I was there in the bin area near my flats and we had a  torch going through the bins like a naughty little fox. I found it in the end.”

Speaking of his nightmare week, he continued: “I ­forgot to pay a parking fine  two years ago and got a letter from the bailiffs. You wouldn’t believe how much the interest is now.”

If that wasn’t bad enough, Jordan was put in a worse mood yesterday when he weighed himself – a mistake nobody should make in lockdown.

He said on Radio 1: “I’ve got more rolls than Greggs. After lockdown, no more ­eating like a ten-year- old. I need to grow up.  I’m a grown man. It’s nothing short of pathetic.

“Post-lockdown it’s going to be all grapefruit, kale, and broccoli.”

I’ll believe it when  I see it.

JESSIE J is teaming up once again with the ­songwriter  she penned some of  her ­biggest hits with.

Claude Kelly  co-wrote Domino, Price Tag and Wild with the Brit singer, as well as Miley Cyrus’s Party In The USA, and now the talented duo are working together again for her sixth album.

Claude told me: “Jessie is one of my best friends and she is working on music. Although we are ­separated because of Covid, we have had some writing sessions via Zoom. I don’t know what the final tracklist will be for the album but I do believe we’ve done a few special things that will make it on there.”

As a music mastermind who has also written major singles for Britney Spears, Whitney Houston and Christina Aguilera, Claude has now ­created his own girl group.

He said: “I’ve been focused on launching The Shindellas.  Think of your favourite girl bands like Destiny’s Child, it’s that ­calibre. I don’t say that lightly, given I’ve worked with some pretty fantastic people. They’re the next big thing.”

Toe-tall Nessa

SHE has already nailed touching her toes, but ­Nessa ­Barrett will be touching the sky with the release of her new song.

The US singer has teamed up with Blink-182 drummer Travis Barker and alt-pop star JXDN for La Di Die.

Speaking about the single, out yesterday, Nessa said: “It touches on how fame is dramatised and something most people wish for, when in reality, it’s a dark and evil place.”

If you like early Avril Lavigne, you’ll love this.
