Who Is Peyton Manning? Wiki, Age, Height, Net Worth, Wife, Ethnicity

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, April 16, 2024

Peyton Manning, nicknamed “The Sheriff,” is a former American football quarterback who played in the National Football League (NFL). Because of his numerous career record-breaking achievements, many consider him to be one of the best and greatest quarterbacks in NFL history. He retired after 14 seasons with the Indianapolis Colts and four seasons with the Denver Broncos.

Peyton Manning Biography

Peyton Manning, the modern-day legend, is the son of former American football quarterback Archie Manning and Olivia Williams, who gave birth to him in 1976. He has two brothers, Cooper Manning, an investor in a New Orleans energy investment firm, and Eli Manning, a New York Giants quarterback. He attended Louisiana’s Isidore Newman School before enrolling at the University of Tennessee.

Peyton Manning’s Wife, Marriage

Peyton Manning married Ashley in 2001, and the couple has twin daughters and sons, all born in 2011. He is a Christian and an author in addition to his football career. He and his father co-wrote the book “Manning: A Father, His Sons, and a Football Legacy.”He is also the founder of the ‘Payback Foundation,’ which helps disadvantaged children.

Peyton Manning Age, Height, Weight

Peyton Manning was born on March 24, 1976, and is 47 years old as of today. His height is 1.96 meters and his weight is 104 kilograms.

Career

Peyton Manning began his football career while still a student at Isadore Newman School. For his prowess in the game, he was named champion and player of the year, and he became the most sought-after high school player, with about 50 colleges recruiting him.

During his college career with the University of Tennessee Volunteers, he became the all-time leading passer and won 39 of 45 games, breaking the SEC career win record. He has been inducted into the Tennessee Athletics Hall of Fame, the Tennessee Sports Hall of Fame, and even the College Football Hall of Fame as a result of his successful college career.

Manning’s professional career began in 1998 when he was selected first overall in the NFL Draft by the Indianapolis Colts, where he spent 14 seasons. He later joined the Denver Broncos for the final four seasons of his career, retiring on March 7, 2016.

He is regarded as the greatest passing quarterback of all time, having set records for touchdown passes and passing yards. He is also the only player in NFL history to have appeared in four Super Bowls.

Peyton Manning’s Achievements and Awards

Peyton Manning has received dozens of awards and achievements, including three Bert Bell Awards (2003, 2004, 2013), NFL Comeback Player of the Year (2012), three NFL passing yards leaders (2000, 2003, 2013), four NFL passing touchdown leaders (2000, 2004, 2006, 2013), induction into the Indianapolis Colts Ring of Honor, and the ESPY Icon Award.

Peyton Manning’s Net Worth in 2023

Peyton Manning is estimated to have a net worth of $230 million as of November 2023. Manning has made money through football since he joined the Colts and received an $11 million signing bonus on a contract worth around $48 million. A contract extension was now worth $99 million, and he received a signing bonus of $26 million.

After joining the Broncos, it is estimated that he earned around $40 million during his tenure there. Peyton Manning’s net worth has also been boosted by the $12 million he has earned from endorsement deals with companies such as Sony, Gatorade, MasterCard, Reebok, Direct Tv, and others.
