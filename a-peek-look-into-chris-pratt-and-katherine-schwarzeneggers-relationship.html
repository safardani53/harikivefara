A Peek Look Into Chris Pratt And Katherine Schwarzeneggers Relationship

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, June 3, 2024

Today marks the third wedding anniversary of Chris Pratt and Katherine Schwarzenegger. The pair resorted to Instagram to express their feelings for one another. The actor has been busy promoting his Jurassic World Dominion film, but he took a break to celebrate his marriage with his wife, who had just given birth. The 42-year-old actor described Katherine Schwarzenegger as his “strong, attractive, brilliant, wonderful, organized, tough, loving, caring wife” on Instagram. He went on:

“I’m a fortunate guy.” God is wonderful. The kids and I are overjoyed to have you. You do so much to make our lives better. Thank you very much. I adore you. We adore you!”

Chris Pratt posted a photo of himself with his wife, kids, and bunnies. He also shared an Instagram story in which the pair looked to be celebrating their marriage in a low-key way by going on a coffee date.

The 32-year-old also praised her hubby on her Instagram account. She then referred to Pratt as her “love angel face” and went on to say:

“I love you more and more each day, and I consider myself very fortunate to share my life with you.” Thank you for being the finest husband, partner, and parent imaginable. You make me laugh, make me feel loved and supported, and you make things better.”

The author also mentioned Chris Pratt as the “greatest postpartum carer,” making soup for her “every night.” She continued, saying:

“The greatest delight is seeing you adore me and our family.” “Congratulations on your anniversary!”

Chris Pratt and Katherine Schwarzenegger’s relationship

The pair began dating in 2018, only a year after Pratt’s divorce from Anna Farris was finalized. Pratt and Farris have been married for eight years and had their son Jack together in 2012. In June 2018, Pratt and Schwarzenegger hinted at a connection when they were photographed riding together in a Santa Barbara Park. They met via the author’s mother, Maria Shriver, according to US Weekly. According to the source:

“They’re in a relationship.” They’re head over heels in love.”

A month later, the couple, together with Pratt’s son Jack, went to church. The pair were sighted together by the paparazzi in the months that followed, making their relationship Instagram official. Chris Pratt hinted at his connection with Schwarzenegger in November 2018 when he retweeted a message made by the author on Instagram. The two were discussing the flames in California.

The pair announced their engagement on Instagram in January 2019. The two hugged as the newly engaged fiancée displayed her huge engagement diamond. In the caption, the Guardians of the Galaxy actor wrote:

“I’m so glad you said yes, sweet Katherine!” I’m overjoyed to marry you. I am proud to live courageously in faith beside you. “Let’s go!”

The pair married in June 2019 at the San Ysidro Ranch in Montecito, California. According to US Weekly, “about 60 people” were invited. The couple had their first child, a girl called Lyla Maria Schwarzenegger Pratt, in August 2020. Elouise Christina, the couple’s second child, was born last month.
