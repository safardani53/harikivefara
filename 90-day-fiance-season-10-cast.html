90 Day Fianc Is Back for Season 10 Let's Meet the Cast

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, March 21, 2024

The TLC hit where fiancés have 90 days to wed aka '90 Day Fiancé' is back for Season 10. Let's meet the new couples who are part of the cast.

The Gist:

  • TLC's hit show 90 Day Fiancé is back for Season 10.
  • The new season premieres on Oct. 8, 2023.
  • TLC released the cast information to get fans excited about the upcoming season.

It has been over a year, but the wait is finally over and 90 Day Fiancé is coming back! That's right folks, Season 10 of the hit TLC show is premiering on Oct. 8, 2023, and it can't come soon enough.

While we still have a bit to go until the premiere date, TLC did drop the cast for Season 10 getting us excited for the drama that's about to take over our lives every Sunday night.

Without further ado, let's meet the 90 Day Fiancé Season 10 cast!

Let's get to know the ‘90 Day Fiancé’ Season 10 cast!

90 Day Fiancé is the show that follows Americans and their foreign fiancés as they take 90 days to decide if they want to spend their lives together.

Let's meet the cast (aka couples) that will appear during Season 10.

Jasmine and Gino

If Jasmine (36) and Gino (52) look familiar, that's because they are! The couple originally appeared on the 90 Day Fiancé spin-off, Before the 90 Days. Jasmine is originally from Panama and has officially made the move to Michigan for Gino.

Sophie and Robert

Sophie (23) hails from the United Kingdom and is moving to Los Angeles, Calif. to be with Robert (32) and to hopefully make a living on the scene. The couple originally met on social media.

Manuel and Ashley

Manuel (34) and Ashley (31) actually met years ago when Ashely was studying abroad in Ecuador, where Manuel is originally from. The couple broke up when Ashley moved to the U.S. afterward but always kept in touch and eventually started a long-distance relationship before moving to New York.

Nick and Devin

Nick (30) is from Australia while Devin (23) lives in Arkansas. The pair met on a dating app and were together for three weeks before deciding to go for the K-1 visa. It took two full years before it was finally approved!

Justin and Nikki

Justin (36) is moving from Moldova to be with Nikki (47) in New Jersey. The couple originally met on a dating site in Moldova, but when Justin learned that Nikki was trans, the pair broke up. He has now changed in mind and the pair are back together.

Anali and Clayton

Anali (26) and Clayton (29) originally met on an app, but not a dating app, a language app! The two hit it off right away and Anali is now moving from Peru to Kentucky to be with Clayton.

Citra and Sam

Citra (26) and Sam (30) also met on a dating app! Citra and her father will be moving from Indonesia to be with Sam in Missouri.

Make sure to catch new episodes of 90 Day Fiancé premiered Oct. 8, 2023, at 8 p.m. EST on TLC.
