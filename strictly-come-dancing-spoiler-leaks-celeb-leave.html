Strictly Come Dancing fans furious as spoiler leaks which celeb is second to leave competition

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

STRICTLY Come Dancing fans were unimpressed tonight as the results of this week’s competition were leaked online.

The second elimination is set to air tomorrow, but a notorious mole has once again spoiled the result.

The Sun has decided not to name the celebrity, but Strictly viewers have expressed their upset over which two couples were in the dance off and who ends up getting sent home after Movie Week.

Tweeting about the results, one person wrote: “That #StrictlySpoiler is depressing tbh.”

While another person moaned: “Why are my favourites always in trouble?” and someone else simply commented: “Noooo.”

During the results show the scores from the judges on the first show and the public vote will be combined 50-50 and announced.

Then the two couples with the lowest number of points from a combination of judges’ scores and the results of the vote will go into a dance-off.

After they both perform their dance again, the judges will decide which couple will stay in the competition.

The dance off and dumping is filmed straight after the live show, but is not shown until Sunday night.

Last year the mole leaked which celebrity was voted off of the show each week, and it looks like they're intent on doing the same again for 2023.

Furious Strictly bosses were reported to have launched a probe to find out who was behind the spoilers.

A source said: "Enough is enough, it isn't fair on the fans, they look forward to tuning in to see who will be dancing together."

This week Irish TV presenter Angela Scanlon and her professional partner Carlos Gu topped leader board with their Charleston from the film Damn Yankees.

Jody Cundy found himself at the bottom after a difficult week or training, while Nikita Kanda struggled with her confidence and came second bottom.

The DJ is odds-on at 4-5 with bookmaker Coral to leave weekend following her performance with Gorka Marquez.

In the outright market for the trophy, Nigel Harman is the 7-4 favourite, while Bobby Brazier is second best at 3-1.
