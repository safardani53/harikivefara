Jake Abel Compares ‘The Host’ To ‘Twilight’

Jake’s new film, The Host, is Stephenie Meyer‘s first post-Twilight film, so of course some Twilight comparisons are to be expected. Especially between Jake and the Twilight leading men, Taylor Lautner and Robert Pattinson. Although he doesn’t want to be compared to them, he admits he’s in good company.

“A lot of people expect us to knock the Twilight cast,” Jake explains in the new issue of VMAN. “But Taylor Lautner is a really nice kid. Robert Pattinson, I couldn’t imagine the tremendous amount of pressure he was under — and he earned my respect when I saw him fight for certain projects. Water For Elephants is a great novel, the movie is what it was. That’s not his fault. That’s the risk; we’re the face of projects.”

Ouch! That last comment was a little harsh!
