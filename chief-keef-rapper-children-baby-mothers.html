How many kids does rapper Chief Keef have?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, February 16, 2024

CHICAGO rapper Chief Keef has nine children by nine different women.

Several of the musician's baby mothers are demanding he cough up child support payments after falling behind on his installments.

Who is Chief Keef?

Keef, who was born Keith Farrelle Cozart, is a 25-year-old rap artist from Chicago.

He was only 17 when he released his 2012 debut album, Finally Rich, and he has been co-signed by the likes of Kanye West, 50 Cent, Wiz Khalifa and Gucci Mane.

He's credited by many for pioneering so-called "mumble rap" and "drill" sub-genres of rap.

Since his debut, his discography now compromises of over 20 mixtapes and albums.

The rapper has had a troubled past with the law, and was arrested for aggravated assault and heroin distribution in his early years.

He was banned by Chicago Mayor Rahm Emanuel from playing a live show in his hometown as a result of his criminal record.

How many kids does he have?

Chief Keef has nine children with nine different women.

He claims that four of his children are under the age of five.

According to TMZ, one of his baby mothers demanded that the rapper be thrown in jail for failing to meet his child support requirements.

Keef initially told the court that he had five children, all with different mothers, but it was later revealed that he had four additional children whose mothers were also all seeking child support.

Chief Keef, who also goes by the alias “Sosa,” was already ordered to pay each of the first five woman $564 per month, starting in January.

He is reportedly only making $9,000 a month in income but has listed $9,000 in expenses.

Who is his baby mama Slim Danger?

Slim Danger is an adult actress who hooked up with the rapper after setting her sights on him at one of his concerts.

Slim claims she met Chief Keef after sending him a message and a video of herself on Instagram.

She allegedly told him she was attending one of his concerts in hopes that the two would hook up. 

It's unclear whether the two met for the first time that night or if they met at a later date.

On her podcast, Slim claimed she “manifested” the pregnancy saying: “I didn’t get pregnant yet, so I was like, ‘I gotta do a little bit more manifestation. I got to get pregnant.”

She revealed she discovered she was pregnant once she went to California and let Chief Keef know. 

“I told him. I was like, ‘Yeah, I’m pregnant’ and he said, ‘Join the crew.'”

The two are reportedly not together but are instead working on co-parenting their four-year-old son Zinc. 

Earlier this year, the porn star made controversial allegations about NFL wide receiver Odell Beckham Jr's sexual preferences.

What is his net worth?

Chief Keef was allegedly worth $1million according to Celebrity Net Worth.

In 2013, Keef signed a lucrative three-album deal that was reportedly worth more than $6 million before taxes, managers fees, lawyers, and expenses.

He was dropped by his label the following year for failing to meet an album sales quota.
