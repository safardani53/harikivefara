Showing it off!

Jada Pinkett Smith left little to the imagination in the sexy ensemble she wore to support her hubby, Will Smith, at the 16th Annual Latin Grammy Awards in Las Vegas on Thursday, Nov. 19.

Beneath the transparent top, Pinkett Smith put her toned torso on display. “I love dressing up and playing different parts in public, so you can imagine what I’m like in private,” the Gotham star joked to Howard Stern in June about showing off her body for her husband.
