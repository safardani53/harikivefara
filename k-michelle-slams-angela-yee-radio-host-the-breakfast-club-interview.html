I Aint F***ing With You Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, February 1, 2024

“What I am going to say, Angela, is that, even with you, you’re a woman,” K. Michelle began her rant. “I think that I’ve been very cool and very supportive of you. The interview was over. The credits were running. You brought that back up… It didn’t get brought back up. I’d seen it. You brought it back up and then you was being all instigative and he said he slept with me, you was all, ‘Ew, you nasty’…At the end of the day, I ain’t f**king with you for that.”

Ouch! Looks like Angela totally broke girl code — and K. is only calling her out for it…in front of all Angela’s listeners! “There are certain things, especially as a woman, you don’t want these bodies on you, you know what I’m saying?” K. continued. “Especially from a Soulja Boy,” she said. “It wasn’t even about them. It was kind of like, ‘Come on, Angela. Like, dude. These n****s right there. They don’t give a f**k, but you being a woman, and you see me, you brought that back up.”

But just what previous convo is K. Michelle referring to? During their appearance on Angela’s show, Maino, Soulja Boy, and even Uncle Murda, 35, joked about K. Michelle’s lady parts having a bad smell. “She got that stink coochie, I call it the K. Michelle. Sometimes I just speak what’s on my mind. I know people who did certain things with her. My man said it was not right,” Uncle Murda said. Now that’s NOT ok! We can see why K. is angry with Angela for not having her back. And according to The Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta star, Angela not only didn’t stick up for her, she INSTIGATED the entire conversation!

“You brought it up,” K. Michelle said to Angela. “I’m telling you, you brought it up. You sat there and chuckled and you don’t know what that might have done to me. If I wasn’t a b***h who was dranking her drank, cashing her checks, it might would’ve f**ked up my whole motherf**kin’ day. My thing with you is the fact that you are friends and family of the label. You were at my listening party. You knew that album was coming out.”

Finally K. left her with these last words: “Well, what you did was f**ked up.” And to prove her point, K. didn’t even pose with the host for her after-interview photo! But it seems like Angela is actually the one who got the last word — at least for now — as she tweeted directly after the interview: “no one will ever bait me into an argument when those love and hip hop cameras are rolling.” So did K. start the drama for ratings on her TV show? Honestly we may never know. But one thing’s for sure, the drama was REAL!
