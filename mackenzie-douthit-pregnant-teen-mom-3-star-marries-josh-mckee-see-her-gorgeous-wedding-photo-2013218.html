Mackenzie Douthit Wedding Photos: Teen Mom 3 Star Marries Josh McKee

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, January 1, 0001

Here comes the bride! Teen Mom 3 star Mackenzie Douthit said "I do" to her high school sweetheart, Josh McKee, in a romantic country-themed wedding on Saturday, Aug. 17 — and Us Weekly has all the exclusive details!

Radiant in a rhinestone-studded white corset gown with a ruffled, high-low hem, the 18-year-old 16 and Pregnant alum, now Mrs. McKee and pregnant with her second child, married her love of three-and-a-half years at The Barn at Timber in Pittsburg, Kansas. Her 20-year-old groom, in keeping with the theme, dressed for the occasion in Wrangler jeans, a suede vest, and a white cowboy hat.

As previously reported, the couple exchanged vows in front of 200 guests, including six bridesmaids and six groomsmen. The bride's BFF, Cayla Bowman, served as maid of honor, while McKee's brother Caleb filled the role of best man.

After the ceremony, the high school sweethearts and parents to Gannon, 23 months, hit the dance floor. A deejay was on hand to play all of their favorite country songs, and guests feasted on the reality star's favorite meatballs, fruit, cheese, and turkey and ham.

The Teen Mom 3 star and McKee appeared together on season 4 of 16 and Pregnant. They're expecting their second child in 2014.
