Kylie Jenner slammed for 'overfilled' lips by top plastic surgeon who says she 'lost sight of what i

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, September 27, 2024

NOT only has Kylie Jenner been called out by fans for her "overfilled" lips, but now, a professional plastic surgeon has done the same.

Jonathan Kaplan, a plastic surgeon in San Francisco, used Kylie as an example in a recent TikTok.

The surgeon, Dr. Jonathon Kaplan (@realdrbae) shared his thoughts on a photo of Kylie from the side that showed off her lips looking extra plump.

"NEVER go to an injector that is they themselves overfilled," Kaplan began as he warned those wanting to get lip injections.

"If you have an appointment with an injector that has lips like this," he pointed to the photo of Kylie, "That means they lost all concept of what is aesthetically pleasing and they're going to do the same thing to you.

Whether it's the Kardashians and body modification in general, people have lost sight of what is really beautiful."

A Reddit user commented, "She got called out by a professional."

"Kylie was the reason I wanted lip fillers and now she’s the reason I don’t," admitted a second.

"The risk/reward is just so bad with filler. When done right, it can definitely enhance someone’s looks, but does it make them beautiful to have plump lips? I don’t think so.

You are already good looking or not. So is the overfilled/migration risk worth a small upgrade? No way," explained a third.
