What happened to Real Housewives of Miami star Guerdy Abraira?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, May 7, 2024

REAL Housewives of Miami Guerdy Abraira joined the cast of the hit show in season four.

Abraira tearfully revealed her stage one breast cancer diagnosis to fellow cast members in the second episode of the show's sixth season.

What happened to Guerdy Abraira?

The Real Housewives of Miami star shocked her friends and co-stars by revealing her breast cancer diagnosis while dining at The Key Club in Miami.

In episode two of the Bravo show's sixth season, Champagne Confessions, Abraira is dining out with fellow RHOM stars Adriana De Moura, Nicole Martin, and Julia Lemigova.

Fighting back tears, Abraira said: "I went in for one mammogram. That turned into an MRI. That turned into one, two, three biopsies."

"And I'm just gonna say it, I have breast cancer."

Her co-stars expressed their shock but quickly rallied to support Abraira.

Martin said Abraira was going to "Guerdy-fy cancer," referencing the star's famous catchphrase.

The foursome then toasted to Guerdy beating cancer.

Fellow cast member Julia Lemigova's wife, Martina Navratilova, also recently battled stage one throat cancer and breast cancer, revealing her diagnosis in January 2023.

Navratilova announced she was cancer-free six months later in July 2023.

In a statement on her Instagram, Abraira urged her female followers to get health checks regularly, which she says was key in discovering her cancer early on.

“I am lucky that this breast cancer was discovered at an early stage – it is still scary of course, but I have love and support from those around me and that alone is the fuel that I need.”

“For those who do not get health checks regularly, I urge you to. Your life depends on it.”

Abraira announced she was cancer-free in November 2023 at BravoCon in Las Vegas.

"I’m cancer-free, officially!" she told a roaring crowd. 

"I’m very excited, and I’m so blessed, and I literally wake up every morning counting my blessings and making it count."

What is Guerdy Abraira's net worth?

Abraira's net worth is reportedly somewhere between $2 and $3 million.

Besides being a reality TV star, Abraira is also an entrepreneur and runs her own event-planning business called Guerdy Design.

She was named Harper Bazaar's Top Planner in the World in 2020.

Her income is likely to go up as her fame rises.

In October 2023, she signed with CGEM Talent for management representation.

The firm also represents fellow Bravo stars Cynthia Bailey and Shereé Whitfield from Real Housewives of Atlanta.

Is Guerdy Abraira married?

Guerdy Abraira met her husband, Russell Abraira, when the two were sophomores in high school.

The duo started dating during their senior year.

They later went to a local college together, and dated for six years before getting engaged.

The high school sweethearts got married on November 16, 2002, in North Miami Beach.

Abraira posted a touching tribute on her Instagram last year celebrating the pair's 20th anniversary.

The couple have now been married for 21 years, and together for nearly 30 years.

Besides being a fan favorite "House Husband," Russell is also a fire chief and firefighter for the city of Miami.

Abraira says she credits her husband's love, care, and support for making her cancer journey easier.

In an interview with People, she said: "I just don't even know how he did it...he's the best caretaker that I could have asked for."

She also added, teasing, "And not only that, but he's also very good-looking."

How can I watch Real Housewives of Miami?

Audiences can watch the show every Wednesday on Bravo at 9 pm EST.
