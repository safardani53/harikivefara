Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, May 19, 2024

ALDI has confirmed that it is no longer carrying a popular brand of crunch wraps.

The retailer responded to a fan on Twitter who could not find the Reduced Sodium Whole Grain Wraps at her local store.

"It’s been months since I’ve seen these wraps at @AldiUSA did you guys stop selling them?

While Aldi responded, it was to deliver bad news.

"Sorry to disappoint, but they have been discontinued," Aldi USA answered next to a broken heart emoji.

The fan did not take the confirmation well.

MORE FOOD NEWS

While Aldi wrap buyers have one less option, Walmart shoppers can look forward to seven new ice cream options.

The retailer has partnered with hip-hop icon Snoop Dogg to exclusively carry Dr. Bombay Ice Cream.

The new line is based on the Dr. Bombay character the rapper originally introduced in a popular line of non-fungible tokens (NFT).

Dr. Bombay is a joint venture of the music icon and Happi Co.

The Ice Cream flavors include:

  • Bonus Track Brownie
  • Cocoa Cream Cookie Dream
  • Iced Out Orange Cream
  • Rollin' In the Dough
  • S'more Vibes
  • Syrupy Waffle Sundaze
  • Tropical Sherbet Swizzle

The flavors were crafted based on Snoop Dogg's tastes.

 "Ice cream is more than just a snack to me; it's a way to chill, relax, and get happy. That's exactly what I want Dr. Bombay Ice Cream to do—bring a smile to your face and ease your mind," Snoop Dogg said.

The Dr. Bombay character is key to the brand's identity.

"Fans will also notice that my sidekick, Dr. Bombay is the name, face, and persona of this brand. That's because he's like a son to me and you always want your kids to be more successful than you are, that's my goal in building this lifestyle brand – starting with ice cream," he said.

Dr. Bombay Ice Cream became exclusively available at select Walmart locations this week.
