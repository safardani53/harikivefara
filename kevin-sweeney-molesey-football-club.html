Who is Molesey Football Club supporter Kevin Sweeney?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

KEVIN Sweeney was recently caught on camera abusing pro-Palestine protesters on Saturday.

Mr Sweeney is wanted for questioning in relation to racially motivated offences. But who is he and what does he do for a living? Here's everything you need to know.

Who is Kevin Sweeney?

Kevin Sweeney was videoed at Waterloo Station shouting racist abuse towards pro-Palestine protesters.

The clip shows the 61-year-old blasting “terrorist c***” at demonstrators on Saturday.

British Transport Police shared his face on social media along with another three other men they want to speak to about the row.

After the incident, officers turned up on Sunday at his £600,000 semi-detached house to question him.

What does Kevin Sweeney do for a living?

Kevin is an amateur football club secretary for Moseley FC.

The club are part of the Combined Counties league - and he has also played for the veteran's side.

What ties does Kevin Sweeney have to Moseley Football Club?

In 2004, he played in a Chelsea old boys team against Molesey FC team.

He rivalled ex-Blues legends for a charity match at their Walton Road stadium.

What happened at Waterloo station?

The football fan was with three other men as they confronted a group of pro-Palestinian supporters on Saturday evening at the conclusion of the march on Armistice Day.

Chaos unfolded over the weekend when a group of protesters were ambushed by a mob of angry men in Waterloo station.

Mr Sweeney was filmed yelling obscenities at the group calling them 'terrorist c****'.

The incident ended when a member of staff at the station intervened.

In a statement, the British Transport Police said: "Officers are looking to speak to the individuals in the images who they believe may have information that could help their investigation."
