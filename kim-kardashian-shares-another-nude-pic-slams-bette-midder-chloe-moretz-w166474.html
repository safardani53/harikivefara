Kim Kardashian Shares Another Nude Pic, Slams Bette Midler, Chloe Moretz

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, March 16, 2024

Taking advice from Yeezy? Kim Kardashian took to Twitter later on Monday, March 7, to lash out at Bette Midler and Chloë Grace Moretz for daring to criticize her choice to share naked selfies— and to share another one!

The 35-year-old reality star shared a photograph (her most NSFW zones blanked out with modesty barriers) via her Instagram account the day before, and caused a social media storm with the new, curvaceous pictures.

And while some people applauded the mom of two for daring to bare what they initially assumed was her postbaby body (in fact, Kardashian later revealed the snap was a throwback to the year before when she weighed "25lbs less") a significant number of commenters (Midler and Moretz included) questioned whether she should be stripping off now that she's a married mother.

"Kim Kardashian tweeted a nude selfie today," Beaches actress Midler, 70, wrote. "If Kim wants us to see a part of her we've never seen, she's gonna have to swallow the camera." (Kardashian previously made an infamous sex tape with ex Ray J in 2003, and posed completely nude for Paper magazine last year.)

Hugo star Moretz, 19, was in agreement. "@KimKardashian I truly hope you realize how important setting goals are for young women, teaching them we have so much more to offer than just our bodies," she added, before retweeting Midler's comments.

Seems Kardashian wasn't in the mood to take her criticism on the nose — and jumping on Twitter in a move more reminiscent of her loquacious husband, Kanye West, she not only blasted back at her critics, but proved herself to be unrepentant by sharing yet another full-frontal naked photo!
