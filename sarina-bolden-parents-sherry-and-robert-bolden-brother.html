Sarina Bolden Parents Sherry And Robert Bolden- Brother

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, February 7, 2024

Sarina Bolden’s parents, Sherry and Robert Bolden are technicians at Tesla Motors. Just like his elder sister, Sarina’s younger brother Kameron also plays soccer. 

Bolden’s parents are extremely proud of all that she has accomplished and will always be there for her.

They are frequently seen attending their daughter’s games, showing their support. Robert’s Facebook feed is filled with Sarina’s accomplishments.

The professional soccer player, Sarina Isabel Calpo Bolden, plays for A-League Women Club Western Sydney Wanderers.

She started playing soccer at Milpitas High School in California and later played for Loyola Marymount Lions until 2018.

Her club career started in 2020 in Taiwan Mulan Football League. She later represented San Francisco Nighthawks (2021) and Elfen Saitama in Japan (2021-2022).

The United States born previously represented the United States U23 team. However, she currently plays for the Philippines women’s national team.

Moreover, the 27-year-old lifted the 2022 AFF Women’s Championship with the Philippines. 

Sarina Bolden Parents: Sherry And Robert Bolden

The professional soccer star was first born to her parents, Robert Bolden (father) and Sherry Bolden (Mother).

Robert works as a PC seats Supervisor at Tesla Motors. Previously, he worked at New United Motors Manufacturing Inc.

Robert went to Glen High School and refers to himself as a ‘digital creator’ on Facebook. He celebrates his birthday on July 13.

Sarina’s mother, Sherry, completed her education at Laney Community College in California. Later, she worked as an Electronics Technician in Transcontinental Northern CA for 1 year.

Having worked in an aerospace company ‘Space System/Loral,’ Mrs. Bolden has been working as an Equipment Maintainance Technician for Tesla Motors since 2014.

Brother

Sarina Bolden has a younger brother Kameron Bolden. He was born on November 18, 1999, in Milpitas, California, the same town where his elder sister was born.

The younger Bolden studied at the same school as his sister (Milpitas High School) and later graduated with a Major in Business entrepreneurship in June 2018.

Kameron started his professional soccer journey at San Jose State University and joined UC Riverside in 2018.

Later, he played for SJS Spartans in California in 2020 and moved to Stallion FC in February 2023.

The Center-Back is currently not linked with any club.

Additionally, Bolden also has two cousins, Jalen Brown and Ryanne Brown, who, like Sarina, are professional soccer players.

Jalen plays as a forward for Stumptown Athletic in NISA, and Ryanne plays as a defender for OL Reign of the American National Women’s Soccer League.
