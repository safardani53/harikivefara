How many kids does NBA YoungBoy have?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 1, 2024

NBA YoungBoy has developed a cult following after writing and releasing tracks from prison.

On September 28, 2022, the 22-year-old rapper announced the birth of his 10th child.

How many kids does NBA YoungBoy have?

On September 4, 2022, NBA YoungBoy shared a photo via social media showcasing a pregnant woman, causing fans to speculate that he was expecting once more.

On Wednesday, September 28, the rapper confirmed the birth of his 10th child via social media.

He shared a picture of himself feeding his son with the caption: "We got left today for a little but it’s cool’ Top #newborn".

He and his fiancée, Jazlyn Mychelle, welcomed their first child together, a daughter, last year. The pair share a total of two children together.

According to HipHopDX, YoungBoy reportedly has eight other children with seven baby mamas, including five sons and three daughters."

Some of YoungBoy's known children include:

Kentrell Jr

Kentrell Jr is YoungBoy's youngest child.

He was born on January 9, 2021.

Iyanna Mayweather, the daughter of boxer Floyd Mayweather, is the mother.

In April 2020, Iyanna was arrested for allegedly stabbing the mother of one of NBA YoungBoy's children.

Kodi Capri

Houston actress Drea Symone welcomed their daughter, Kodi Capri, on Thanksgiving 2020.

Reports claim she is the couple's second child, born on November 26 of that year.

Drea often shares pictures on Instagram with her daughter, who she tags as Kens.

Armani

Armani is the second child of YoungBoy and a woman named Nisha.

She has an older brother, Kayden.

Kacey

Kacey was born on February 13, 2019.

His birth mother is the social media influencer, Jania Bania.

Taylin

Taylin was born on March 19, 2017.

His mother is a woman named Nia, who is said to have experienced a one-night stand with YoungBoy.

After a paternity test, it was confirmed that Taylin is the rapper's son.

Kamiri

Kamiri is YoungBoy's son with Starr Dejanee.

He was born on July 6, 2017.

Kamiri has a brother, Kamron, who was also thought to be the rapper's son.

In June 2018, YoungBoy made a Facebook post saying one of his sons, Baby K, is not biologically his.

He wrote: "Wanna Be First To Tell Yall I Officially Found Out Baby K Isnt My Child, But The Man I Am Im Going To Keep Raising Him Like He's Mine I Cant Lie It Hurt To Find Out The Truth But Life Goes On."

Kayden

Kayden is the rapper's oldest child, born on July 14, 2016.

His mother is Nisha.

Kayden appeared in his dad's video for the single Kacey Talk, along with his half-brother, Kacey.

Who is NBA YoungBoy?

NBA YoungBoy was born in 1999 in Baton Rouge, Louisiana.

His real name is Kentrell DeSean Gaulden.

He is known professionally as YoungBoy Never Broke Again, or NBA YoungBoy.

As a toddler, YoungBoy broke his neck while wrestling and the injury required him to wear a head brace which left permanent scars on his forehead.

He dropped out of ninth grade to focus on his music career but was arrested for robbery and sent to a detention center in Tallulah, Louisiana.

After he was released, he and a friend reportedly used acts of criminality to pay for studio time.

He was signed to Atlantic Records in 2017, and his debut album, Until Death Call My Name - which peaked at number seven on the US Billboard 200 - was released the following year.

His first chart-topping project came in 2019 with AI YoungBoy 2, which debuted and peaked at number one on the Billboard 200.
