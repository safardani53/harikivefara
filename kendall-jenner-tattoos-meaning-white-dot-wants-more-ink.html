Reveals Meaning Behind White Dot Hollywood Life

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 1, 2024

Kendall writes that Kim’s opinion doesn’t matter when it comes to the Estee Lauder spokesperson’s decision to tat up. “Even though Kim always says, ‘Don’t put a bumper sticker on a Bentley,’ I really like tattoos,” Kendall says. “Right now I have two: They’re both in white ink on my two middle fingers. The one on my right hand [formerly a small dot] is a full heart to represent an angel and my left is a broken heart — kind of like the devil side.” Ah, so that’s what happened to the white dot! At the time, the tattoo artist who gave Kendall the ink, named JonBoy, explained why it’s white: “To her, it’s the little things that matter. The white color was because she didn’t want it to show with modeling and all that. It was a fun night,” Jon said.

Kendall added that she doesn’t plan on stopping at just two tattoos. “I definitely want more eventually,” she shared on her site. “I just don’t know what I would get right now. I don’t have anything in my mind.” Whatever Kendall decides to get, we’re sure it will be edgy and meaningful at the same time!
