Donald Trump with a Man Bun is a Sight You Can Never Unsee

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, June 15, 2024

Like many other sane people, Jennifer Lawrence hates Donald Trump.

But the actress may change her mind after viewing the following photo.

Indeed, a unique picture of the Republican Presidential nominee surfaced on imgur Wednesday.

It features Trump with a man bun and Twitter user @FigDrewton actually Tweeted the photo at The Donald himself after Trump went off on Marco Rubio and illegal immigration.

"I watched lightweight Senator  Marco Rubio, who is all talk and no action, defend his WEAK position on illegal immigration. Pathetic!" Trump wrote on the social media site yesterday.

And this photo is what @FigDrewton (what a handle!) sent back to him in return. Pretty amazing.

Trump, of course, has insisted for years that his hair is real, even using a random woman to prove that point earlier this summer.

It’s possible (just possible, mind you) that this picture is not real, that it’s been doctored via Photoshop.

But even so. Could it be that all of Trump’s sexist and racist rants have been worth it just so we could all enjoy the majesty of this man bun?

No? It’s not possible? They haven’t been worth it. Okay. We agree.
