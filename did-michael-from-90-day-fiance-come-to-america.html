Michael and Angela From '90 Day Fianc' Are Rumored to Be Together in the U.S. Now

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Did '90 Day Fiancé's Michael Ilesanmi come to the U.S. to be with Angela Deem after '90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After' Season 7?

Some 90 Day Fiancé couples are part of the franchise for almost as long as their relationships span, and Michael and Angela are one of those couples. We've seen them through all of their highs and lows and now, in the midst of their latest tiff in Season 7 of 90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After?, they seem on the brink of divorce.

Still, some fans think Michael moved to the U.S. after filming, so did Michael come to America after Season 7 of 90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After?, or did he and Angela finally call it quits for good?

Right now on the show, they aren't in a good place, since Angela isn't wild about Michael's social media activity. And, despite Michael's claims about loving his wife, he doesn't want to give up those followers without a fight.

Did '90 Day Fiancé's Michael come to America?

Even though neither Michael nor Angela have publicly shared if Michael is in the U.S. now, one fan did recently share a photo they allegedly snapped of Michael and Angela in a store. And that store appears to be a big-box department store, which means it's a store in the U.S., rather than Nigeria, where Michael is from.

Going by that, it seems like Michael and Angela are not only still together, but also living together. And if that's the case, it's a long time coming for them. Their journey started in 2017 on 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days Season 2. They eventually got married in Nigeria, and in Season 7 of Happily Ever After?, Angela reveals that Michael's K-1 visa was approved for his move to the U.S.

Angela has an issue with Michael being on social media on '90 Day Fiancé.'

Even though it certainly looks like Michael and Angela are together now and living in America, they've had a rough road to get there.

On the show, Angela accuses Michael of cheating on her with women on social media due to comments that she sees as flirtatious. If Michael agrees to delete his Instagram, all will be forgiven.

However, Michael explains to Angela, he makes money from Instagram and he doesn't want to give up that stream of income. Outside of that, Michael doesn't work. He tells Angela to fork over a few thousand dollars and he'll get rid of social media.

Naturally, this doesn't go over well with Angela. So she does what any wife would do and flies across the world to surprise her husband and demand to see his phone in real life.

Now, Angela has moved on from Michael's social media to his phone contacts and she's sure Michael has feelings for a female friend whose information is saved in his phone. Whether or not Angela actually has something to worry about, her continued jealousy is an ongoing issue for the couple.

But if Michael really is in America now, apparently they make it through yet another hurdle in their relationship.
