Hi-Rez Studios is a privately owned and independent American video game company. The company was founded in 2005 by Erez Goren and Todd Harris. Hi-Rez Studios games include the shooter from the critically acclaimed lineup Global Agenda, Tribes: Ascend, MOBA Smite third-person shooter, Paladins hero shooter, and secondary shooter 3 Rogue Company. In 2012, Hi-Rez Studios was recognized by Game Developer Magazine and Gamasutra as a Top 30 video game developer. Hi-Rez is the current owner of Metaltech licenses, including Battledrome, Earthsiege., Starsiege, CyberStorm series and Tribes series. With the exception of Battledrome and CyberStorm, the games were released as free software by Hi-Rez on October 30, 2015
