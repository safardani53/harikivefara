Cute Pic! Tom Cruise, Katie Holmes, Suri Go Ice Skating

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 14, 2024

Ice queen in training?

Suri Cruise looks the part in her pink skirt and figure skates! On Saturday, the 5-year-old hit the ice with parents Tom Cruise and Katie Holmes in a Pittsburgh skating rink. See more pics from the fun family outing.

On Friday, Cruise, 49, and Holmes, 32, celebrated their five-year wedding anniversary. Suri was born seven months before her parents' lavish wedding at Odescalchi Castle in Bracciano, Italy.

The family is staying at the Fairmont Pittsburgh while Cruise films One Shot, and a source tells Us Weekly that the trio rented out an entire floor of the hotel for themselves!
