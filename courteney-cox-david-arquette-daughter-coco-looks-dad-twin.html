Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, May 1, 2024

COURTENEY Cox and David Arquette's daughter has just celebrated a milestone birthday.

Fans cannot get over how much she looks like her father's twin in a new rare Instagram photo.

Courteney posted a photo with her daughter, Coco, to celebrate her 18th birthday.

In the photo, the 58-year-old cuddled up next to her daughter while they were standing on a beach.

She shared two other photos of Coco- one of her when she was little and another of her holding her dogs.

"Happy 18th birthday coco!" she captioned the post.

In the first new photo, Coco is a spitting image of David, with his same eyes, and smile.

She celebrated her 18th birthday on June 13.

SHE'S GOT PIPES

Back in August, the Friends actress posted a video on her Instagram of her playing the piano, a man playing guitar, and Coco singing.

Coco belted out a rendition of Adele's Chasing Pavements and Courteney looked up in awe at her daughter.

She hit every note flawlessly and proved that she has tremendous talent.

Proud mom Courteney captioned the post, "I love my Coconut's voice."

The Scream actress wore a chambray shirt with skinny jeans and heels while Coco sported a colorful crop top and wide-leg pants.

Although the teen usually resembles her dad, she looked like a mini-Courteney in the clip.

She might have a singing career in the future.

NEW LOVE?

Back in July 2020, Coco was seen introducing Courteney and her boyfriend, Johnny McDaid, to a male, who could have possibly been a new boyfriend.

The family was seen eating out at the Italian restaurant Giorgio Baldi in Santa Monica.

Courteney wore blue jeans and a long-sleeved white sweatshirt while carrying a water bottle and wearing a face mask.

Her daughter wore a white tank top, black pants, and high-heeled boots.

MORTIFIED BY MOM

In April of this year, Courteney talked to InStyle about Coco being her biggest critic.

She revealed that her daughter has an eye for social media trends and she lets Courteney know when she is out of the loop.

"Coco gets really embarrassed by a lot of the things I put on Instagram," the actress told the publication.

"Sometimes, I'll find something on TikTok and put it on Instagram and she'll say, 'Mom, that is so over.'"

Courteney went on to say that one time when she did a dance trend, Coco told her that she was "mortified."

"When I look back, I'm kind of mortified," The SAG Award winner admitted.
