Strictly Come Dancing 2023 live Angela Scanlon eliminated from showas Bobby sails through afte

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, May 11, 2024

'I'm so proud'

Carlos Gu had nothing but praise for Angela Scanlon following her Strictly exit last night.

Gu said she has worked "so hard" during her time on the dance competition.

And added: "Look at her now - I am so proud, her sense of humour and we had so much fun everyday - she cracks me up laughing."

'I'm gutted, really gutted'

Speaking after being dumped from Strictly last night, Angela Scanlon said she was "gutted" to say goodbye, but her experience on the show was "incredible."

She said: "It's been honestly incredible, I'm gutted, really gutted that it's finished now, but it has been the most wonderful experience.

"I have made the best friend in this man (Carlos).

"We have had so much fun, he is such an incredible guy.

"He has supported me and picked me up and made me laugh so much.

"I don't know what to say, it's been magical honestly - all of those people up there.

"My husband at home, my two little girls and all my friends."

What did Bobby say about his emotional performance?

Bobby Brazier paid an emotional tribute to his late mum, Jade Goody, over the weekend.

His striking performance left fans in tears as honoured his beloved mother.

Speaking before taking to the dancefloor, he said: "This dance is a tribute to my mum. The story we're going to tell is the story of her and I.

"My mum was Jade Goody and she was a famous TV personality who became a household name.

"She passed away when I was just five. It changed my life completely.

"I'd love to spend a day with her, just to see where I get my smile from."
