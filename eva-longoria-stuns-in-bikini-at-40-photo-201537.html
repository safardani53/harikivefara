Eva Longoria Stuns in Bikini at 40: Photo

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, February 25, 2024

40 and never looked better! Eva Longoria’s curves were on full display in a teeny blue bikini on Friday, July 3.

The Desperate Housewives alum looked flawless while vacationing on the beach in Marbella, Spain. The actress paired her suit with aviator sunglasses and hoop earrings.

Longoria is in Spain to host the Global Gift Gala, which raises funds for multiple charities, including her own.

During an interview with pal and Extra’s Mario Lopez this past April, the Devious Maids executive producer gushed about turning the big 4-0.
