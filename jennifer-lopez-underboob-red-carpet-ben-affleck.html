Jennifer Lopez flashes underboob as she poses in very unusual red carpet dress with Ben Affleck

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

JENNIFER Lopez showed off a whole lot of underboob as she stepped out with husband Ben Affleck in Los Angeles last night.

The Hollywood power couple dressed to impress at Nya Studios for Elle's Women in Hollywood Celebration.

JLo's bespoke outfit looked like she'd been covered in liquid metal and stopped abruptly just above her chest.

The 54-year-old beauty wore her hair scraped back and dangly earrings capped off her eye-catching outfit.

Actor and director Ben, 51, had salt and pepper stubble and opted for a more traditional velvet suit ensuring all eyes were on his other half.

The couple wrapped an arm around each other as they posed for photos before JLo stepped away and modelled her head-turning outfit from multiple angles.

Multi-talented Jennifer has been at the top of her game both musically and filmically for more than two decades.

While she's done it all, from chart-topping hit to box office smashes, she still has lofty ambitions.

She told Elle: “I want to keep evolving. And whether I’ll take the helm and direct my own film at one point is a possibility. I’ve talked about it. I’ve been offered to direct a couple of things, and I’ve turned [them] down, just because of time constraints and things like that.

"I can’t tell you, ‘I’m going to direct three movies, and I’m going to do this, and I’m going to do that.’ But I can say that I will keep evolving as an artist and as a performer and producer and in all of the ways to create the projects that I decide to be involved with—and I hope that that never stops.”

Ben and Jen's close display comes month after reports of tension between them behind the scenes.

Sources claimed Ben was growing tired of JLo’s diva demands for everything to be “perfect” in the quest to find their dream home, while they were also been pictured arguing at red-carpet dos as he took issue with her revealing tops.

They must have worked past that last point though as it proved to be one of Jen's most revealing looks to date.

She recently told Vogue that she was very much in control of every aspect of her life, including her fashion choices.

She said: “I’m finally at the point in my life where I love every part of myself unapologetically.

"Every part of me, my body, my voice, my choices, even, like, my mistakes. All of it made me who I am and got me to where I am today. Ben wants me to understand my worth and know my value. I feel even more relaxed and comfortable, which makes me feel even more beautiful than I have ever felt with someone else.”
