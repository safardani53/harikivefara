9 Best Men's Christmas Suits 2022 | The Sun UK

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, May 26, 2024

TAKE the Christmas jumper tradition one step further this year with a head-to-toe festive look.

Christmas suits are a great way to combine fancy dress and sharp tailoring for men who want to look smart for Christmas festivities but have a bit of fun with it too.

Typically consisting of a printed festive jacket and trousers, men's Christmas suits are a favourite for work parties and New Year's Eve bashes.

You can also find accessories like ties, waistcoats and even festive footwear to complete the look.

The appetite for men's Christmas suits has become big enough that certain brands like Opposuits and Dobell have established themselves as dedicated purveyors of patterned and novelty suits for any occasion, especially Christmas.

When it comes to choosing a festive print for your Christmas suit, we say, go big or go home. From festive lights to Santa prints, Brussels sprouts to baubles, all you need is some confidence.

  • Wicked Costumes Blue Snowman Christmas Jacket & Tie Fancy Dress, from £15.95 at Amazon - buy here

Not everyone wants to do the head-to-toe men's Christmas suit look, which is why this snowman print jacket and tie combo is a nice alternative that you can throw on over a tee and trousers so it's festive without being overkill. It's also great value for money.

'This jacket and tie was a great option for our office party, a talking point.' writes one wearer.
