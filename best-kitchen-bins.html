11 Best Kitchen Bins 2020 | The Sun UK

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, October 3, 2024

EACH household in the UK produces over a tonne of waste each year, so we all need a sturdy bin to help carry the load.

Choosing a bin isn't the most exciting job though, it's a practical buy - not one most particularly want to break the budget over.

It's not something many want to spend precious time on either.

But whether you prefer a handy pedal to flip the bin lid up, or a cheap and cheerful number that does its job and nothing extra, there are plenty to choose from.

To keep it simple, we've done the hunting for you.

With seven styles of bin on offer and prices varying from £6 to £179, it should be simple to pick your favourite from our selection below.

  • Brabantia Bo Touch Bin, 36L, Platinum, £149 from John Lewis - buy here

We know we said a bin is all about practicalities, but this is an exception.

Crafted in sleek steel with chic mid-century angled legs, it's sure to bring a stylish dimension to your kitchen.

But it's not all about style: the soft-touch closure and easy-to-clean removable inner bucket make those everyday chores a little bit easier, too.

It's a lot of cash to splash on a bin, but we think it's worth it.

2. Best cream kitchen bin: Habitat Alto Cream Kitchen Bin

  • Habitat Alto cream kitchen bin, 32l, £35 from Argos - buy here

The only downside of this bin is you have to manually lift the lid, but let's not get too lazy shall we?

The upside is that with such a simple design, there's no push or pedal mechanism to break, so it's sure to last.

It's a chic vintage design, especially in cream, but it comes in plenty of colours, so you can make it fit your kitchen's theme.

Reviewers are with us when it comes to style, with people also raving about how the retainer ring doesn't rip the bag when you remove it.

3. Smartest kitchen bin: John Lewis Joseph Joseph Titan Compactor Bin

  • Joseph Joseph Titan Compactor Bin, 30L, £179 from John Lewis - buy here

We do love when household appliances are smart.

This bin's compactor will crush your waste down so you can fit three times as much rubbish in as a regular bin of the same capacity.

That, of course, means taking out the rubbish less - always a winner.

The worry with 90l of rubbish in your house is the smell, but it has an odour filter you can keep replacing, so a stinky bin isn't an option.

You're going to use less bin liners too, so it's lighter on the wallet in one way at least.

4. Cheapest kitchen bin: Argos Tontarelli 25L Swing Top Bin

  • Tontarelli 25L Swing Top Bin, £5 from Argos - buy here

This is a no-frills bin.

You get what you see, which is an easy swing lid bin that's made of plastic.

Plastic is not great environmentally, but it's durable as it's wipe-clean, so it's likely to last a long time.

Its minimalist design will complement most kitchens, too.

5. Best kitchen recycling bin: Amazon VonHaus Recycling Pedal Bin

  • VonHaus Recycling Pedal Bin with Double Compartments, 60l, £89.99 from Amazon - buy here

Recycling is non-negotiable - it needs to be done for the sake of the environment and lack of landfill space.

This dual-compartment bin makes it easy to clean and do, with one side for regular rubbish and the other for recyclables or food waste.

No more glass jars hanging around the sink, or empty wine bottles lined up near the trash, you can use this dustbin to keep things neat.

It's got a huge capacity, but it's not ideal if you're limited on space.

6. Best kitchen pedal bin: Amara Simplehuman Brushed Steel Butterfly Pedal Bin

The first great thing about this bin is its sleek metallic exterior, set off by black details.

Because of its foot pedal, it's much more hygienic than the average bin which opens by hand.

Plus, it's been made to last so the pedal shouldn't need replacing any time soon, while the inner bucket is easy to remove, so it'll stay mess-free and be easy to empty, too.

7. Best black kitchen bin: Dunelm Bling Black Glitter 12L Pedal Bin

  • Bling Black Glitter 12L Pedal Bin, £14 from Dunelm - buy here

We're proving ourselves wrong again here - it turns out bin shopping is more exciting than it first appears.

This black glittery bin is one to add a bit of sparkle to your kitchen.

It's not all looks though, as it boasts a hygienic pedal mechanism and soft close lid.

8. Best statement piece bin: Amazon Wesco Spaceboy XL

  • Wesco Spaceboy XL, £146.43, Amazon - buy now 

This really is the best statement piece bin in the business.

Although the price of even its smallest pedal bin pushes the £100 mark, they're built exceptionally tough, and should last for decades.

And if you haven't noticed, the Spaceboy XL looks like a 1950's cartoon rocket.

It doesn't do anything fancy - no sensors, compactors or odour filters here - but it's made from thick rolled steel.

9. Best auto-opening bin: Sense Touch-Free Sensor Bin 50L

  • Touch-Free Sensor Bin 50L, £45, Sense - buy now

Hygienic and sleek looking with a slimline silhouette, this touch-free sensor bin is perfect for your modern kitchen.

Made with non-corrosive iron with a smooth finish that's easy to clean.

It even has a removable inner bucket and standard bin bags.

There are seven different colours available.

10. Best versatile bin: Amazon Joseph Joseph Totem 48

  • Joseph Joseph Totem 48, £106.59, Amazon - buy now

Stylish and durable, it has a coated stainless steel body and fingerprint-proof stainless-steel lid.

The carbon filter lid helps eliminate any odours.

It's the perfect bin for separating recycling or storage.

Great for couple who generate a lot of waste.

11. Best bin for large families: John Lewis Simplehuman Rectangular Recycling Bin

  • Simplehuman Rectangular Recycling Bin, £179.99, John Lewis - buy now 

Simplehuman's dual compartment pedal bin makes it easy to neatly sort your rubbish and recyclables in one convenient, space-efficient space.

It's perfect for large families who need more room to recycle their waste.

The strong and wide steel pedal with Lid Shox technology ensures a smooth, silent close every time.

Which is the best kitchen bin?

The best kitchen bin will depend on your kitchen's style.

If you seek something with the highest level of hygiene, check out Simplehuman Brushed Steel Butterfly Pedal Bin.

But if you want to add more colours to your interior take a look at Elements 40 Litre Slim Printed Bin.

What size are kitchen bins?

Kitchen bins vary in size a lot.

The smallest bins can have 10L capacity and can go up to 60L.

Make sure you've decided bin's place so you can get the perfect size for your kitchen.
