Mama June fans fear Pumpkin and husband Josh have secretly split after spotting 'sign' in her new Ti

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, March 7, 2024

LAURYN Efird, known as Pumpkin, has raised the question of whether or not she and her husband Josh have broken up.

The daughter of Mama June shared a new TikTok video that may have hinted at a potential split from the fellow reality star.

The new video showed an emoji shouting the phrase: "she's probably the ugly ex."

The eerie sound playing in the background featured the words: "You won't die, but you'll wish you could."

The clip then cut to a selfie video of Pumpkin in the car.

The TLC star was seen rocking a pair of glasses and an orange knit top while her hair was styled half-up-half-down.

Some fans assumed the video was a dig at the Mama June: From Not to Hot star, believing the couple had split up.

The two have been married since April 2018, and on May 19, 2022, Pumpkin gave birth via C-section to the twins at a Georgia hospital. 

The pair also share son Bentley and daughter Ella.

Alongside their children, they have custody of Pumpkin's sister Alana Thompson, also known as Honey Boo Boo.

A SERIOUS SPLIT?

Taking to the TikTok video's comments section, fans of the TLC star have been voicing their concerns over her relationship.

One fan asked: "Are her and josh still together?"

"Does this mean you're single?" another inquired.

"Are y’all broken up?" penned a third.

"Wait her and josh broke up," another wrote alongside a wide-eyed emoji.

However, some fans have chimed in to say that the pair are still together, with one noticing a specific detail.

"Her wedding ring is still on and they are still tg," they wrote.

Another joked: "You were never the ex were you? Don’t make the crew come after josh and whoop him."

Others reposted the video on Reddit with their own opinions.

"I don't think they split she's posting this for attn for sure though," one user wrote.

"When did they supposedly break up? He was just there the other night when pumpkin and Alana were yelling insults and cussing at each other on tiktok live," another inquired.

Another wrote: "She isn’t single it’s a trend on TT people do!"

While Pumpkin has yet to respond to any of the comments directly, it can be inferred that she may have been referring to a previous relationship.

TATTOO SLANDER

Recently, Mama June's son-in-law was slammed by fans after he revealed his new tattoo on social media.

Josh posted to his Instagram Story showing off his new ink and was quickly criticized for the piece.

The reality star reposted an image from his tattoo artist.

The artist wrote: "Big cover-up for [Josh]. One more session for background and stuff."

Pumpkin's husband posed topless for the photo and turned his head away from the camera.

The new colorful ink covered his entire chest, reaching from shoulder to shoulder, and down to his ribcage.

The design featured autumnal leaves and green leaves with berries, with an animal centerpiece.

The brown animal looked to be growling fiercely, showing off its fangs and bright red mouth.

A fan reposted the image to Reddit, where many others pitched in to make their thoughts on the tattoo clear.

One critic joked: "Oliver Peck and Chris Nunez (Ink Master) would tear this to pieces."

"I’m really hoping they’re getting this stuff done for free in exchange of promo because they should be so embarrassed and ashamed of themselves," added a second.

There was also plenty of confusion surrounding the animal ink: "Whoever did his tattoo is skilled but not an artist. Unless the dragon is supposed to look like a gorilla."

"I thought maybe it was supposed to be a bear," someone responded with a laughing emoji.
