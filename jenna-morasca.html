Career

Jenna began her career as a bikini model. She rose to prominence after starring in Survivor: The Amazon in 2003. She was also able to win the show’s million-dollar big prize. Morasca also participated as a co-host on CBS’s Survivor Live in 2005. She has since hosted the full five seasons. In addition, she is a professional wrestler. She made her professional wrestling debut in March 2009, when she appeared on Total Nonstop Action Wrestling’s TNA Impact! In terms of her acting career, she has appeared in Off-Broadway (2003), The Scorned (2205), The Watcher (2011), and many more. Morasca and her then-boyfriend, fellow Survivor champion Ethan Zohn, competed in the 19th season of The Amazing Race in 2011.
