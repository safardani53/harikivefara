Brian Doyle-Murray is an American actor, comedian and screenwriter. As the brother of actor / comedian Bill Murray, he has appeared with him in a number of films including Caddyshack, Scrooged, Ghostbusters II, Groundhog Day and The Razor's Edge. He starred in the TBS sitcom on Sullivan & Son, where he played the rude Hank Murphy. He also appeared in Nickelodeon's animated series SpongeBob SquarePants as The Flying Dutchman, in Cartoon Network's original animated series My Gym Partner as trainer Tiffany Gills, The Marvelous Misadventures of Flapjack as Captain K 'nuckles, in a recurring role, like Don Ehlert in the ABC sitcom The Middle, and Bob Kruger in the AMC comedy, Lodge 49. Doyle-Murray was nominated for three Emmy Awards in 1978, 1979, and 1980 for her work on Saturday Night Live in the Primetime Emmy Award for Best Writing for a Diverse Show, Music, or Comedy. Two other younger brothers, Joel and John, are also actors.
