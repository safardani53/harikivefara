This massive purple caravan is an incredible 13 metres long and 2.5 metres high and was designed for a couple that requires two different areas to work from home.

Based on the price of other bespoke vans made by the firm, this one would cost around £100,000.

Walking through the proper front door reveals a luxury kitchen with a copper sink, four burner gas cooktop, oven and even an extractor hood.

The owners chose wooden flooring and walls for their modern look and because they can be cleaned easily.

This caravan also has a proper dishwasher installed in the kitchen and a massive fridge.

Next to the kitchen, there’s a living area which has enough space for a full-size sofa.

The owners are planning to put a dining table next to the kitchen for meal times.

Then, under the window in the kitchen, they plan to put a table to work from home.

To maximise the space inside, they have installed storage within the staircase for their shoes.

On the first floor, you’ll find a bathroom with a walk-in shower and eventually, a washing machine will go in too.

There's also a master bedroom with a huge wardrobe where the couple can store plenty of clothes.

The bed has hidden compartments too which can be used to store extra stuff.

Next to the master is another smaller room that can be transformed into an office.

A third bedroom on the second floor has space for only one bed.

YouTube users are amazed by this van and most commented that they love it.
