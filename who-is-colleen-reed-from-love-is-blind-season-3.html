Who Is Colleen Reed From Love Is Blind Season 3?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, January 1, 0001

Season 3 of Love is Blind debuted on Netflix lately. On October 19, 2022, the first four episodes of the renowned reality TV series were published. Thirty fresh singletons entered the pods, eager to take the leap and fall in love blindly. Colleen Reed, a 26-year-old ballet dancer from Dallas, Texas, is one of them. In the episode, she proposed to Matt Bolton.

According to the official synopsis for Love is Blind,

“The pods are now open,” Vanessa and Nick Lachey like to joke. Or, at the very least, they will be when Season 3 of Love Is Blind starts on October 19. Those pods will naturally be filled with 30 individuals taking a chance and expecting to fall in love sight unseen, however, only a few will eventually get engaged without ever seeing their beloveds. It takes a rare cast to go headfirst into Love Is Blind, and this ensemble is remarkable.”

Season 3 of Love is Blind has published a teaser ahead of its launch, which shows multiple proposals and wedding preparations. The preview also shows one of the cast members heading down the line but having second thoughts. Viewers will have to watch the series, which is now available to stream on Netflix, to find out whether they made it or not.

Colleen Reed, from Season 3 of Love is Blind, is a digital public relations expert.

Colleen was born in Easton, Pennsylvania, in 1995, and presently works as a dancer with Ballet North Texas in Dallas, Texas. Colleen’s passion for ballet began at an early age, according to Cinemaholic, when she began studying under the tutelage of Oleg Briansky and Mirelille Briane. She subsequently went on to study at Pennsylvania Youth Ballet with Karen Knerr.

Colleen has performed at a number of ballets throughout the years, including the Boston Ballet, the Orlando Ballet, and the Nashville Ballet. She earned a degree in Public Relations from the University of Oklahoma in 2018. Colleen earned her Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Ballet Performance the following year.

Colleen, according to her Netflix profile, is eager to meet the love of her life and believes he is in the pods. Her longest relationship lasted almost five years. She also feels that when one finds their person, they have an immediate connection.

“I’m a big believer that when you meet the ‘one,’ you will get an immediate sensation, and I haven’t felt it yet.”

Colleen also thinks that the experiment would help her locate her forever person by removing distractions. She has around 2000 Instagram followers. Her social media highlights include her summer vacations, dances, and pals. Most of her postings also feature photos from her ballet performances, beach visits, and outings with her pals. The first four episodes of Love is Blind season 3 are now available on Netflix. The following episodes of the program will be released on Wednesday. Previous seasons and episodes are also available to view in order on the streaming service.
