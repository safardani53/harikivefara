Benjamin Aaron Shapiro is a conservative American political commentator and media presenter. At 17, he became the youngest country conservative in the United States. He writes for Creators Syndicate, Newsweek and Ami Magazine, is the Honorary Editor of The Daily Wire which he founded and hosts The Ben Shapiro Show, a daily political podcast and live radio show.
