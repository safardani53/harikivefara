Laguna Beach's Taylor Cole Marries: See Her Dress, Costar Bridesmaids!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Please say someone played Hilary Duff or Blink-182 at the reception! On Friday, Oct. 2, Taylor Cole, one of the stars of Season 2 of Laguna Beach, married her longtime love Kevin Simshauser in a gorgeous outdoor wedding at the La Quinta Resort & Club in La Quinta, Calif. 

The Laguna Beach High School alum, who is now a professional photographer, wore a stunning Jenny Packham gown with capped, beaded sleeves, and pulled her blonde hair back in an updo. 

The bride’s father walked her down the aisle as she carried a giant bouquet of flowers. 

“There aren’t words to describe the most incredible weekend of our lives. We are so full of love, smiles and amazing memories. I am one happy WIFE! #palmsimswedding,” Cole posted on her official Instagram page. 

By her side were six of her best gal pals, including Alex Murrel (now Johnson) and Morgan Souders, who both appeared on the MTV series with her. The bridesmaids wore flower crowns and floral-printed dresses in different styles. 

“My best friend is now a Simshauser @taylorcolephoto #palmsimswedding,” Murrel wrote via Instagram. “Had the best time celebrating my best friend @taylorcolephoto and her new husband @kevsims13 this weekend #palmsimswedding.”

Souders added, “Our TAY TAY is getting married #palmsimswedding.”
