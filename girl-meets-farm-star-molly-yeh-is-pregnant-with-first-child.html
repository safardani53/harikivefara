'Girl Meets Farm' Star Molly Yeh Is Pregnant With First Child

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, February 10, 2024

This bun is safe in her oven! Girl Meets Farm star Molly Yeh is pregnant with her first child with husband Nick Hagen, Us Weekly can confirm.

The Food Network personality, 29, shared the happy news with her Instagram followers on Saturday, November 10. “AND make way, baby bump comin through!!!!” Yeh wrote in her caption, adding heart, smiley-face and baby bump emojis. “See ya in march, offspring!!!!!”

In the gallery of photos, the chef points to her barely there belly with a look of pure glee and Hagen gestures toward the baby, too.

Yeh also teased that her bump would appear on the season 2 premiere of Girl Meets Farm. “Pie, donuts, goulash, brussels sprouts and this gigantic sugar beet in a jar as a party decoration,” she wrote on her Instagram Story on Saturday. “It’s all on Girl Meets Farm tomorrow!!!! See you at 11a/10c on @foodnetwork.”

She added: “p.s. there will also be … A BABY BUMP!!!!! … coming end of march!!”

The couple celebrated their anniversary in December 2017. “Three years and he still hasn’t farted in my presence,” Yeh wrote on Instagram at the time. “HAPPY ANNIVERSARY TO MY FAVORITE HUSBAND YET!!!! I love you!!!!!!”
