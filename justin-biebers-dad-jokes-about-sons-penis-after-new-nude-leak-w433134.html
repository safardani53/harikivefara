Justin Biebers Dad Jokes About Sons Penis After New Nude Photo Leak

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 11, 2024

Not again. Justin Bieber’s dad, Jeremy, joked about his son’s penis for the second time following the “Sorry” singer’s recent nude photo leak.

As Us Weekly previously reported, Justin was photographed swimming buck naked with a group of girls during a trip to Hawaii earlier this week. The NSFW, full-frontal images were later published by the New York Daily News on Friday, August 5.

Although the “Cold Water” crooner has yet to acknowledge the new photos, his father took to social media on Saturday, August 6, to crack jokes about the size of his son’s nether regions.

“My boy,” the 41-year-old dad of three wrote in a since-deleted tweet alongside a cactus emoji, which many social media users interpreted to represent male genitals.

Jeremy then shared an Instagram photo of his native Toronto’s famously tall CN Tower, which appeared to be a nod to Justin’s penis size as he tagged the heartthrob in the caption with a smiley-face emoji.

Moments later, the Canadian, who shares Justin with ex Pattie Mallette, posed for a selfie next to the 1,815-foot tower, which he also tagged his son in.

“I don’t give af but a real father doesn’t say anything about his son’s d–k size,” one user commented, while another added, “This is just weird.”

This isn’t the first time Jeremy quipped about Justin’s manhood in a cringeworthy social-media post. After a different set of nude photos of the “Baby” singer leaked in October 2015, Jeremy called himself a “proud daddy” on Twitter before asking his son, “What do you feed that thing?”
