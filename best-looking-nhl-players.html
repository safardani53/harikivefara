1. Henrik Lundqvist 

  • Date of Birth: March 02, 1982, Age: 41 Years Old

Finally, we have 6 feet and 1-inch tall Henrik Lundqvist on the top of the list of the 12 best-looking NHL players in the World.

Henrik Lundqvist is a former professional ice hockey goalkeeper from Sweden.

Lundqvist was a member of the New York Rangers of the National Hockey League for his entire fifteen-year career (NHL). 

He guided the Swedish men’s team to their second Olympic gold medal in the 2006 Winter Olympics in Turin, Italy.

Additionally, he won the Honken Trophy three times in a row in 2003, 2004, and 2005.

Later on, he also went to win won the Gulf Pucken (Golden Puck) and the Guldhjälmen (Golden Hjälmen) in 2005, two of the most prestigious trophies in Swedish Ice Hockey (Golden Helmet).
