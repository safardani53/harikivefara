Tottenham star Dele Alli shows off incredible tattoos including Super Mario, Rick and Morty and Stew

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 26, 2024

TOTTENHAM star Dele Alli has shown off his new tattoos including ones from TV shows Family Guy and Rick and Morty.

The 25-year-old's latest body artwork was revealed via an Instagram video from teammate Sergio Reguilon.

The Spaniard zoomed in on a number of Alli's tattoos and praised him for his boldness.

Reguilon added the caption: "What a tattoo my friend."

Among the most visible tattoos was one of Stewie, a well-known character from American comedy Family Guy.

There was also a detailed depiction of legendary video game figure Super Mario, with Grand Theft Auto also represented in the form of a 'Vice City' tattoo.

And popular Netflix show Rick and Morty was another famous TV show drawing tattooed onto Alli's body.

Alli captained Spurs in their pre-season friendly against his former side MK Dons last week.

Nuno Espirito Santo's side won the match 3-1, with Alli netting the Lilywhites' second goal of the game.

The former England man will be hoping he can feature more for Spurs this season than he did in the last campaign.
