Where do Chantel and Pedro live now they are divorced? Wed 8 November 2023 10:57 Yasmine Leu

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Divorces either end on good or bad terms and for Chantel Everett and Pedro Jimeno, it’s clearly the latter. The Family Chantel stars made their TV debut on 90 Day Fiance season 4 and became the first couple to earn their own spinoff show. After falling in love online, the standout pair’s honeymoon period ended quickly. Fast forward to 2023, Chantel and Pedro are heading for divorce and have slammed all reconciliation rumors. The final step came with the selling of their family home in April.

Where do Chantel and Pedro Jimeno live now after bitter divorce?

Pedro Jimeno hails from the Dominican Republic but has chosen to stay in Georgia, Chantel’s home state, after the split.

Chantel, meanwhile, is now based in Atlanta for her job as a nurse injector at a medical spa. However, as a travel nurse, she is usually on the go. In November, she boasted about her “dream job”, revealing she was driving to Tennessee for the day.

The couple purchased their first home together in January 2022 for $290K. According to InTouch Weekly, the two-story property, which is listed under Everett’s name, boasts five bedrooms, three and a half bathrooms, and a landscaped lawn. Located in Lawrenceville, Georgia, the 2627 sqft property features an open-plan style and high ceilings.

The home was sold for $417K in March 2023. The season 5 premiere saw the couple reunite for the first time in nine months to discuss the joint sale.

“The moment I sat down, I felt like I made a mistake to show up and grace someone with my presence, who had treated me so bad and evil,” she told cameras.

With Pedro obtaining his real estate license in July 2021, the 32-year-old was disappointed that he couldn’t save them the $25K commission by putting it on the market himself.

Pedro splashes on $275K bachelor pad

Jimeno is officially ready for new beginnings as the realtor bought a new house months after selling the first family home.

The bachelor pad is located in Jefferson, Georgia, meaning it is about 50 minutes away from his previous house, and is just over one hour away from Atlanta.

The $275K ranch property is smaller than before, at 1588 sqft. It features four bedrooms and three bathrooms.

New romance rumors are brewing

Both Chantel and Pedro are seemingly ready to get back into the dating game after both stars were seen spotted with potential partners.

In February, Jimeno posted a photo of himself sitting with two women in a restaurant booth, captioned: “New beginnings.” Chantel, meanwhile, fueled relationship speculation with rapper Waka Flocka Flame after they posed together at a college event.

The 32-year-old was even linked to Drake, who reportedly reached out to check on her amid the divorce.
