Blac Chyna slams Kylie Jenner for taking daughter Dream on Kobe Bryant death helicopter without p

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, April 6, 2024

ROB KARDASHIAN'S baby mama Blac Chyna slammed Kylie Jenner for taking her daughter Dream for a ride on the same helicopter that killed Kobe Bryant without her permission.

After news broke of Kobe's unfortunate passing on Sunday, Kylie took to Instagram to offer her condolences before saying she had ridden with the same helicopter pilot in the past.

Blac Chyna's lawyer talked to OK! Magazine and claimed the model was livid after she found out Kylie had risked her child's life by taking her on a helicopter ride.

Lawyer Lynne Ciani also claimed the cosmetics mogul had used Kobe's death as a publicity stunt.

Lynne Ciani commented: "Chyna has become aware that Kylie Jenner is using the tragic deaths of Kobe Bryant, his beautiful daughter, and seven other precious souls to profess her 'distress' that she and Dream had ridden on the same helicopter with the same pilot prior to Sunday’s horrific crash.

"What Kylie failed to disclose was that Chyna never gave Kylie permission to take her precious daughter Dream on a helicopter ride and that Chyna never would have given Kylie that permission."

Most recently, Kylie had taken allegedly taken Dreams on a helicopter ride in November 2019.

The lawyer continued: "No parents should find out after-the-fact that their child has participated in a dangerous activity without their permission.

"Chyna was distraught to learn that Kylie had taken Dream on that helicopter ride in November 2019. Chyna voiced her strong objections to Rob and insisted that it never happen again.

"Chyna sends her heartfelt prayers and condolences to all of the families impacted by yesterday’s tragedy.”

Blac Chyna has a daughter with Kylie's brother, Rob.

The two are currently battling it out in court over custody of their daughter, Dream, with Rob claiming that he's scared for Dream's safety.

Rob first tried to get the court to order drug tests every time Blac Chyna wanted to visit Dream, but now he's asking for full custody due to her violent behavior and aversion to drugs.

Court order papers claimed that Dream has been in the arms of a nanny while the model goes after the nanny. It also talked about how she's left the toddler alone for hours while she does drugs with strangers.

The only Kardashian brother also claimed Blac Chyna has been teaching their daughter sexual dances and expressions.

After news broke of Kobe's helicopter crashing in Calabasas, Kylie took to Instagram - like many celebrities - to pay her respects for the basketball player, his daughter, and the six other people who perished.

She wrote: "Rest in peace... and prayers to these families. I still can't believe this.

"That was the helicopter I would fly on from time to time with that pilot, Ara. He was such a nice man. Hold your loved ones close."

Kobe and Gianna Bryant were two of the passengers on the helicopter crash that also killed assistant coach Christina Mauser, baseball coach John Altobelli, his wife Kerri and daughter Alyssa, Sarah Chester and her daughter Payton, and pilot Ara Zobayan.
